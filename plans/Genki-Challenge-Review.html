Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will consolidate ALL Challenge language across the whole level — I like…! · Do you like carrots? · Do you have any pets? · What noise does a …? · This is my…! · Do you have any brothers or sisters? — and show what they can do in a happy team quiz.
  • Subsidiary. A song medley of all 6 Challenge songs; an achievement worksheet (mixed review); the flagship team quiz with an animated reward (Review only). Genki big-book favourite.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; keep it celebratory, not testy — every child succeeds; both teams cheer & win something; every child speaks & moves every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • recall & use language from all 6 Challenge topics;
  • answer Speaky's mixed quiz questions in a team;
  • sing along to the Challenge song medley;
  • show their achievements on the review sheet;
  • feel proud: I did it! Challenge level complete!

Classroom management — signals
"Class? — Yes!" — you: "Class!" → children stop & echo "Yes!"
Volume control 🙋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
"Speaky says…" — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
"Freeze!" / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — "5… 4… 3… 2… 1…" with fingers shown.
"Come with me!" 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages (Review)

Same skeleton, review flavour. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). The cumulative Q&A recycle lives at the start; the finale is the flagship team quiz with an animated reward (Review only) + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), "choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair".
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives from the bus-box & shakes hands with each child.
  • Remember time — cumulative Q&A chain across ALL Challenge topics + a fast recall game.
  • Focus / Review — Challenge song medley + quick mixed-topic games (all 6 topics).
  • Table time 🪑 — achievement review sheet (mixed), listen → do → say.
  • Reading time 🟢 — favourite S&S Challenge big-book moment, choral refrain.
  • Final game ⚡ — flagship team quiz with animated reward (World Cup Soccer! / Banana Tree), ends on a high note.
  • Closing — achievement celebration, Thank You! + Goodbye song, "Challenge complete!" sticker.
