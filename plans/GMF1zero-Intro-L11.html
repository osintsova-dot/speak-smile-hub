🦝 Give Me Five! 1 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 11 / Intro L11 — Do you like animals?
Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 05.10–11.10 · Занятие 11✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.11 · большое чтение
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 12 animals and ask & answer “Do you like (dogs)? — Yes, I do! / No, I don't!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.11: большое чтение — праздник: speed columns, big match, re-reading all 5 stories, все 17 tricky words; recycle Do you like…? (зан.7–8) with the new animal words.
  • Personal. Reading day: protect the joyful tone — speed and volume games, zero «проверка чтения» vibes.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name dogs · cats · birds · mice · chickens · sheep · bears · bees · elephants · monkeys · snakes · tigers;
  • survey classmates with Do you like…? and report (Cute!);
  • read CVC word columns at speed and all 17 tricky words in a row.

Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Do you like dogs — про одну собаку или про всех?” → про всех (plural -s).
“sheep — а как shiP?” → sheep долгое и-и-и (овечка тянет)!

Assumptions: From занятия 1–10: Do you like…? chunks are solid; all 26 sounds + capitals; 5 stories already read once each; animal words partly known from cartoons.

Phonics Hero ур.11 — большое чтение 🎉
board → desks

Праздник чтения — стр.28 №1: столбики на скорость. 4 columns (-at·-an / -in·-ip / -og·-ed / -un·-ox). Chorus each column DOWN, then — the trick — UP! Then pairs: read your column to your partner, colour the star ☆. Silly voices: robot, mouse, гигант.

Большое «соедини» — стр.28 №2. Six words (fox, sun, bed, dog, zip, ant) → pictures. Choral read → every child draws lines → partner check (RallyCoach).

Перечитаем истории — стр.29 №1. 5 mini-covers (Ant · Cat · Dog · Fox · Yak): teams of 4 get one story each, whisper-read it together, tick the box — then each team performs ONE line with animal voices 😄.

Да/нет — стр.29 №2 (all vote). It is a dog. It is a van?… — choral read, thumbs vote, circle.

ВСЕ tricky words — стр.29 №3. Two strips (the a I is said he me to you / have we are was go my do no) — читаем подряд «без запинки»: chorus → boys → girls → solo volunteers. Medal ⭐.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — «⚡ Speed reading» на таймер + истории on screen. ◆ Support: read one column + one strip. ▲ Extension: read a column snake-style (вниз-вверх) solo. ✔ Assess: pair re-reads show real speed; note who guesses vs blends.

Round 2 (6). bears (big!), bees (zzz — как звук z!), elephants (trunk arm), monkeys, snakes (sss — как звук s!), tigers (roar — rrr!). Notice: звуки животных = наши phonics-жесты! Flash-check all 12.
