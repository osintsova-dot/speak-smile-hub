🦝 Give Me Five! 1 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 5 / Intro L5 — Numbers 1–12
Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 14.09–20.09 · Занятие 5✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.5 · d·g·o + история про кота
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to count and name numbers 1–12 and answer “How many? — (Five)!” in context.
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.5: sounds d·g·o, dictation + story «История про кота» (все слова уроков 1–5!); recycle can-actions (jump FOUR times!) and feelings inside counting.
  • Personal. Numbers only in context (How many? Jump three times!) — never as a bare recited list.
By the end, pupils can…
  • count objects and claps to 12;
  • answer “How many? — Seven!” about real things;
  • read and write dog, dig, hot, top; read the story «История про кота» — слова уроков 1–5.

Structure

Form
How many + (noun)? Short answer = the number. Plural -s just modelled, not taught.
Pron. watch
three /θ/ — tongue between teeth (not «фри»/«три»); eleven stress e-LE-ven; twelve final /lv/.
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.5 (тетрадь стр.12–14): d /д/ · g /г/ · o /о — губы бубликом!/ · blending dog, red, dig, hot, top, dot · «обведи слово» · диктант dog, dig, hot, top · tricky to, you · история «История про кота» (стр.14).
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“How many fingers?” (show 4) → do I want a colour or a NUMBER? → a number: four!
“b and d — где у d животик?” → слева (bed-мнемоника придёт в ур.7; пока просто следим).

Assumptions: From занятия 1–4: commands, greetings, feelings, can-actions, pronouns, sounds s→m, CVC blending + dictation routine. Russian numbers are automatic — the job is to attach English words to known quantities.

Phonics Hero ур.5 — d·g·o
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.12 №1. Strip s·a·t·i·p·n·c·k·e·h·r·m — twelve sounds! There and back; boys/girls/whisper/LOUD.

Meet d · g · o. d — барабаним: d-d-d! · g — глоток воды: g-g-g! · o — губы бубликом, щёлк выключателем: o-o! Copy ×3, mixed flash.

Blend — стр.13 №1. Windows: /d/ /o/ /g/ — dog! … red, dig, hot, top, dot — air-blend, pictures for meaning.

Trace — стр.12 (desks). Прописи d · g · o · dog · got; whisper the sounds. Then «обведи слово» — стр.13 №2 (dog/dot/dig, map/mop/man, cot/cat/can, ten/tan/tin): read all three together, every child circles, partner check.

Dictation — стр.13 №3. Segment on fingers → write → Show me! Words: dog, dig, hot, top. Tricky strip to · you. Medal ⭐.

Story — стр.14 «История про кота» (settler). Echo-read the 5 frames, finger under the words: Sam has a cat. The cat sat in a hat… ⭐ считаем: how many times is cat in the story? — count in English! (5) — today's numbers, straight into reading.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «c k e h r m d» + история on screen. ◆ Support: blend only dog, hot. ▲ Extension: word 5 by ear (dot). ✔ Assess: Show-me dictation; story star-count = number check too.
