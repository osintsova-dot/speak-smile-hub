Week 21.09–27.09 · Lesson 7✨ NEW · 6 actions + he/she + -s~12 pupils · pairs + 3 teamsPupil's Book p.12 · AB p.9
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 6 ball actions (hit · jump · kick · bounce · throw · catch) and describe what someone does: “He throws the ball. / She catches the ball.”
  • Subsidiary. Sing «He's a superstar!» (CD1 19); consolidate the 3rd-person -s noticed in L3 — now drilled properly; Talk Partners guessing game (CD1 20): action → sport; listening — circle the actions (AB follow-up).
  • Personal. TPR-heavy lesson — plan the freezes; insist on the -s in THIS lesson (it was noticing before, now it's accuracy time).
By the end, pupils can…
  • name & mime the 6 actions;
  • say “He/She + action-s + the ball” accurately;
  • guess a sport from an action clue: “She bounces the ball — basketball!”

Structure
He (throws) the ball. / She (catches) the ball.  ·  game chunk: She bounces the ball. — It's basketball!
Form
he/she + verb+s: throws, kicks, catches (extra syllable!), bounces. “-s belongs to he/she.”
Pron. watch
catches /ɪz/ — two syllables; throw /θrəʊ/ with the tongue-out th; hit short /ɪ/ (not “heat”).
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“I kick — he kick or kickS?” → kicks.
“catches — one beat or two? Clap it!” → two: catch-es.

Assumptions: -s was met in the story (L3); actions run/jump known from GMF1. bounce/throw/catch likely new. Pupils can read short action words on cards. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).

10–19′
Presentation · 6 actions
carpet

TPR present (act. 1, CD1 18). Each action: Throw! Catch! Kick! — you model, all copy, choral ×3. Contrast pair catch/kick held together.

-s drill (the point of the lesson). You mime, class describes: He throws the ball! Boy mimes → “He…”; girl mimes → “She…”. Clap the two beats of catch-es.

▶ Freeze-frame statues. Music on — all mime an action; music off — FREEZE; you tap a statue, class says the sentence: She kicks the ball! Everyone is a statue every round.

💪 Support: chorus only, no solo yet. ⭐ Extension: statue chooses a hidden action — class guesses: He hits the ball!

🃏 Карточки: «-s» окончания (mini) раздай парам — подкладывают под глагол в 3-м лице (he throw+s, she kick+s).

27–36′
Guess the sport
carpet, 3 teams

Model (act. 3, CD1 20). She bounces the ball… It's basketball! — connect action → sport.

▶ Numbered Heads guessing. Teams numbered 1–4. You (then a pupil) give the clue: He hits the ball with a bat… Teams huddle 10 sec, you call a number — that child answers: It's baseball! Point per correct. Everyone must be ready — the number is random.

ICQ: Do you shout the answer or wait for your number? (→ wait!)

Board game · actions
desks, groups

▶ Board game — superstar path. Dice + counters; land on an action cell → sentence with the pictured child: She throws the ball! Landing on a star cell = mime it too. Group checks; first to FINISH wins.

🎮 Interactive (projector): «Ходилка» — actions & sports. One shared class run after the table game; rotating roller, whole class says each sentence. · 🎮 sports-board

✔ Assess: walk the tables — tick your list: who says catch-es with two beats? Who still drops -s?
