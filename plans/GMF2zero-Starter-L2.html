Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 8 school subjects and ask & answer: “What's your favourite subject? — My favourite subject is (English)!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.13 (b): закрепление ai·oa·ie — gap-fill, диграфный диктант, история «Коза и лодка».
  • Personal. Favourite-вопрос уже знаком (colour, з.5) — переносим паттерн на subjects; PE/ICT как буквы-имена.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name 8 school subjects;
  • ask & answer What's your favourite subject? — My favourite subject is…!;
  • write digraph words from dictation (rain, goat, boat, pie) and read the goat story.

Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Favourite subject — все уроки или один любимый?” → один.
“В ответе — my или your?” → MY favourite subject.

Assumptions: Занятие 13: учебник знаком, Jake/Molly знакомы, диграфы встречены. Favourite-паттерн из з.5 переносится. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).

Phonics Hero ур.13 (b) — ai·oa·ie
board → desks

Speed reading + gesture check. Alphabet strip → then flash ai · oa · ie: class answers with sound + gesture. Two letters — one sound!

Blend re-run — стр.34 №1. Windows: rain, snail, goat, boat, pie, tie — faster than last lesson; every child air-blends.

Gap-fill — стр.34 №2 (all vote). Picture + r__n: который друг — ai, oa или ie? Every child shows the gesture → fills the gap: rain, goat, tail, boat, pie, tie. Partner check (RallyCoach).

Digraph dictation — стр.34 №3. Segment on fingers (/g/ /oa/ /t/ — three fingers!) → write → Show me! Words: rain, goat, boat, pie. Tricky strip come · some — flash, chorus, air-write.

Story — стр.35 «Коза и лодка» (settler). Echo-read the 5 frames: Rain on the hills. A goat waits in the rain… ⭐ home: draw The goat sits in the boat. Medal ⭐.
