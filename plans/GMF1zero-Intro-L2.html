🦝 Give Me Five! 1 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 2 / Intro L2 — Good morning + How are you?
Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 07.09–13.09 · Занятие 2✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.2 · i·p·n + первая история
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to greet by time of day (good morning / afternoon / evening / night) and say how they feel: “How are you? — I'm (happy).”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.2: sounds i·p·n, blend real words (pin, tap, nap…), first written dictation and the FIRST story «Моя первая история!»; recycle commands + names from занятие 1.
  • Personal. Keep the занятие-1 rituals identical (same words, same signals) — routine = safety for beginners.
By the end, pupils can…
  • greet with the right phrase for morning / afternoon / evening / night;
  • answer “How are you?” with I'm happy / sad / tired / hungry / thirsty;
  • blend and read pin, tap, nap, sit — and read a whole 5-frame story!

Structure
Good morning! · Hello, hello! · How are you? — I'm (happy / tired / hungry).
Form
I + 'm (am) + feeling word. Contraction only — no “I am” drilling.
Pron. watch
“hungry” vs “angry” (show tummy!); “tired” /ˈtaɪəd/ one smooth fall + yawn gesture.
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.2 (тетрадь стр.5–7): i /и/ · p /п/ · n /н/ · blending pin, tap, pan, nap, tin, sit · «соедини» · первый диктант pin, tap, ant, sit · tricky I, is · история «Моя первая история!» (муравей).
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Is it morning now?” (point out the window) → so what do we say? → Good morning / afternoon!
“I'm hungry — do I want food or sleep?” → food! (rub tummy)

Assumptions: From занятие 1: the 7 commands, Freeze!, “My name is…”, sounds s·a·t and the blending idea. Children are absolute beginners and non-readers — pictures, gestures and chorus everywhere.

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
hungry / angry mixed upAlways pair the word with the gesture (rub tummy); recast, don't correct harshly.
Four greetings overloadAnchor each to a picture corner (sun up / high sun / sunset / moon); drill via movement.
First dictation feels scaryAir-write together first; hint strip is in the тетрадь; celebrate ANY readable attempt.
Karuta slaps get wildRule: gentle flat hand; “hands on head” between rounds; demo softly with a volunteer.
Story: some just memorise, don't trackFinger under each word, chorus reading; ⭐ find-the-word task forces looking at print.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → pupils stop & echo “Yes!”
“WiFi” 🖐 — say “WiFi” + raise a spread hand → pupils silently mirror & tune in.
Volume control — raise/lower your arm = louder/quieter → settler.
“Give me five” — eyes · ears · mouth · hands · feet.
Countdown — “Finishing in 5…4…3…2…1… pencils down.”
“Freeze!” — statue, mid-movement reset.

Phonics Hero ур.2 — i·p·n
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.5 №1. Strip s · a · t: There and back — fast! Boys… girls… whisper… LOUD!

Meet i · p · n. i — мышка: i-i-i! (пальчики-усики) · p — задуваем свечку: p-p-p! · n — самолёт гудит: nnn! Copy sound + gesture ×3; mixed flash.

Blend — стр.6 №1 (шесть звуков — читаем целые слова!). Sound windows: /p/ /i/ /n/ — pin! /t/ /a/ /p/ — tap! … pan, nap, tin, sit. Every child air-blends with a finger.

Trace — стр.5 (desks). Прописи i · p · n · pin · nap — whisper as you write. Then «соедини» task: word → picture (ant, pan, tin, nap), partner check (RallyCoach).

FIRST DICTATION — стр.6 (everyone writes). Listen: /p/ /i/ /n/… pin! — segment on fingers → write → Show me! → reveal. Words: pin, tap, ant, sit. Tricky strip I · is — flash, chorus, air-write.

Story — стр.7 «Моя первая история!» (settler). Chorus the 5 frames, finger under the words: A pan. An ant is in the pan. The ant naps… — их первая прочитанная история! Big cheer 🎉 + medal ⭐.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «s a t i p n»: Station «Блендинг» (sound windows) + история on screen. ◆ Support: dictation with first letter given. ▲ Extension: 5th word by ear (tin). ✔ Assess: the Show-me boards on dictation = live check of every child.

Presentation · greetings + feelings
carpet → 4 corners

Times of day. 4 picture cards in 4 corners: sunrise / high sun / sunset / moon. Walk to each, drill: Good morning! Good afternoon! Good evening! Good night! Then point-and-say from the carpet.

Feelings. 5 face cards with gestures: happy 😊 · sad 😢 · tired 🥱 · hungry (rub tummy) · thirsty (drink). Chorus ×3 each. Model with Speaky: How are you, Speaky? — I'm hungry! Back-chain: you? → are you? → How are you?

▶ Karuta · feelings slap
carpet, pairs

Each pair: 5 emotion cards face-up between them. Hands on heads. Caller (🦝/teacher): I'm hungry! → both slap the right card, faster child keeps a point (gentle flat hand!). Shuffle, repeat — all pairs play at once.

Round 2: children take turns calling inside the pair (RallyRobin) — the caller must SAY the sentence, not just the word. Reset if loud: Freeze!
