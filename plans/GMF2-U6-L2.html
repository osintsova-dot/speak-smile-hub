Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to ask & answer about seasonal habits: “What do you do in (winter)? — We (make snowmen). / What do they do in (summer)? — They (go to the beach).”
  • Subsidiary. Understand the seasons dialogue (CD3 4); family-habits survey; we/they without -s; the do-question machine warmed up (it existed in U1: Do you play…?).
  • Personal. Link back to U1 explicitly (“remember Do you play…? Same DO!”); keep survey answers truthful — real family facts fuel real language.
By the end, pupils can…
  • ask “What do you do in…?” for all seasons;
  • answer with 2 different activities;
  • report about others: “They open presents in winter.”

Structure
What do you do in (autumn)? — We (collect leaves).  ·  What do they do in (spring)? — They (plant seeds).
Form
What + do + you/they + do…? — the two do's (question machine + main verb). No -s with we/they.
Pron. watch
weak do you /djə/; What_do_they runs together; stress the SEASON in the question.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Two do's in the question — same word, same job?” → no: first do = question machine, second do = делать.
“We/They — with or without -s?” → without!

Assumptions: Season sentences active from L1; Do-questions seeded in U1. The double-do confuses — the “question machine” metaphor (у нас уже был DO!) resolves it playfully. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).
