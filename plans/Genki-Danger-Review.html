Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will have consolidated the whole Danger level — I can see a…! · Touch your…! · Put on the eyes! · Give him horns! · My … hurts. · Put on / Take off your…! — used across a song medley & team games.
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time recycle of all earlier levels too; the flagship team quiz with an animated reward (Review only); a mixed review worksheet.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; celebratory tone (уровень пройден!); every child speaks & moves every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name sea animals, body & face parts, monster parts, clothes;
  • use I can see… · Touch your… · Give him… · My … hurts · Put on / Take off…;
  • join every Danger song from memory;
  • play a team quiz answering across topics;
  • feel proud — level complete! 🏅

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌳 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Review finale = the flagship team quiz with an animated reward (only on Review) + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → swim/fly/jump to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives from the bus-box & shakes hands with each child (Hello! How are you?).
  • Remember time — cumulative Q&A chain of ALL past topics + a review game.
  • Focus time — song medley — sing & do EVERY Danger song from the level, re-priming the language.
  • Table time 🪑 — a mixed review worksheet (draw & tell your favourite Danger thing).
  • Reading time 🌳 — children choose a favourite Danger big-book (Let’s Make a Monster!).
  • Final — flagship team quiz ⚡ — World Cup Soccer! with an animated reward, across all topics; ends on a high note.
  • Closing — Thank You! + Goodbye song, achievement check + special “level complete” sticker.
