🦝 GenkiEnglish Preschool · Danger · Lesson 40 — Summer clothes — t-shirt, shorts, sandals, dress, cap
Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
Danger level · L40 · Lesson 7 of 8✨ Summer clothes · Take off…12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to name 5 summer clothes (t-shirt, shorts, sandals, dress, cap) and follow & say Take off your coat! Put on your t-shirt! — with the miming action.
  • Subsidiary. Cumulative Remember-time recycle of greetings, numbers, body & last week's winter clothes — contrasted as "it's hot, take off / put on"; the S&S big-book; a summer/winter sort-&-stick folder sheet.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; clothes always in a command (Take off your cap!, not a bare list); every child mimes every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name t-shirt, shorts, sandals, dress, cap;
  • follow Take off…! / Put on…! with the mime;
  • tell hot from cold clothes;
  • race to change for summer;
  • sort summer vs winter clothes.

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Put on / take off get confusedAlways pair them: It's hot — take off your coat, put on your t-shirt! with opposite mimes.
"sandals" hard to sayTwo clear beats san-dals; the song repeats it; accept a good try.
Mixing summer & winterSun corner (hot) vs snow corner (cold); hold each item & ask Hot or cold?
Miming race → collisionsOne item per turn, quick ROTATION; 🧘 settler + Freeze!
Transitions get chaoticTrain: Come with me! — one signal, one destination.
Classroom management — signals
"Class? — Yes!" — you: "Class!" → children stop & echo "Yes!"
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
"Speaky says…" — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
"Freeze!" / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — "5… 4… 3… 2… 1…" with fingers shown.
"Come with me!" 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello / Good Morning song, "choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair".
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the bus-box & shakes hands with each child.
  • Remember time — cumulative Q&A of everything so far (thumb-up/down like a game), growing lesson by lesson, woven into today's topic.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, Genki topic song, board app, active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheets, a different type each lesson (sort & stick / match & dress соедини / listen & plaster / draw & tell / build & stick / colour-by-command / count), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🟢 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
