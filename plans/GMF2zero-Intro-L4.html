🦝 Give Me Five! 2 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 4 / Intro L4 — How old are you? (numbers 1–12)
Pre-A1 young learners (~8–9 y.o., zero beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 14.09–20.09 · Занятие 4✨ NEW · numbers 1–12 + age~10 pupils · pairs & 2 teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.4 · h·r·m
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to count 1–12 and say their age: “How old are you? — I'm (8) years old!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.4: sounds h·r·m + first-sound sort; recycle pronouns (How old is HE? preview lightly).
  • Personal. Numbers only in context (age, candles, counting real things) — never as a bare list; fingers always up with the words.
By the end, pupils can…
  • count 1–12 forward, backward and out of order;
  • ask & answer How old are you? — I'm (8) years old.;
  • sort words by first sound h/r/m and write hat, red, ham, map.

Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“I'm 8 — is that my name or my age?” → age.
“Three — tongue out or in?” → out! /θ/.

Assumptions: Занятия 1–3 done: commands, names, pronouns+feelings; sounds s→e. Многие «знают» счёт до 10 по-школьному — шлифуем произношение и контекст возраста.

Phonics Hero ур.4 — h·r·m
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.10 №1. Strip s·a·t·i·p·n·c·k·e — nine sounds! There and back, beat your time.

Meet h · r · m. h — руку на грудь, дышим как собачка: h-h-h! · r — трясём тряпочку, рычим: rrr! · m — гладим животик, вкусно: mmm! Copy ×3, mixed flash.

Blend — стр.11 №1. Sound windows: /h/ /a/ /t/ — hat! /r/ /e/ /d/ — red! … hen, rat, ham, map — air-blend, picture for meaning.

Trace — стр.10 (desks). Прописи h · r · m · hat · ram — whisper as you write.

Sort — стр.11 №2 (all vote). Word up (hat, rat, map, hen, mat, red) → read together → every child shows the first sound with its GESTURE (dog-breath / growl / yummy) → write in the right column. Partner check (RallyCoach).

Dictation — стр.11 №3. Segment on fingers → write → Show me! Words: hat, red, ham, map. Tricky strip me (+ quick re-flash of said, he). Medal ⭐.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «c k e h r m d»: «Звуки» + «Блендинг». ◆ Support: sort only 3 words. ▲ Extension: extra word by ear (hen). ✔ Assess: gesture-vote in the sort shows every child's first-sound hearing.

Presentation · numbers 1–12
carpet circle

Build the ladder. Count with fingers 1–10 chorally; add the new floor: eleven! twelve! (жест «двухэтажный» — две руки). Forward → backward → whisper → LOUD.

Out of order. Flash number cards — class shouts + shows fingers. Then ▶ Showdown: pairs get mini-cards; teacher says a number — first to slap it keeps it; all slap every round.

The age chunk. How old are you? — I'm 8 years old! Back-chain: old → years old → 8 years old → I'm 8 years old. Chorus, then Speaky asks 3–4 children.
