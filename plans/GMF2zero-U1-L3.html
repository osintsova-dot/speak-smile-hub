Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to follow, read and act out the story «The very busy week» and talk about another person: “Molly plays tennis on Monday. / She goes swimming.”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.15b · ng·oo — компактный этап (story day); повтор структур L1–L2 в нарративе.
  • Personal. Listen → read → act out: полный story-цикл; драма с ролями для каждого; никакого длинного письма в день истории.
By the end, pupils can…
  • follow the story and read key lines with the class;
  • say He plays… / She goes… about a character;
  • re-read the ng·oo story and write king · moon · book.

Structure
Molly plays (tennis) on (Monday). / She goes (swimming). · recycle: Do you play…? — Yes, I do.
Form
he/she + verb + -s: plays, goes. «-s перепрыгивает на глагол, когда говорим ПРО кого-то» + on + день.
Pron. watch
plays /z/, goes /z/; Wednesday /ˈwenzdeɪ/ — d молчит!; days chant ритмом.
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.15b · ng·oo (тетрадь стр.40) — этап «Phonics Hero — ng·oo (b, light)»: полный ход в Procedure ниже.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Molly plays — я говорю С Молли или ПРО Молли?” → про неё (-s!).
“On Monday — когда или где?” → когда.

Assumptions: L1–L2: спорты + Do you…? активны. Дни недели — новые, вводим чантом до истории. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).

Phonics Hero — ng·oo (b, light)
board → desks

Speed reading — now WITH ng·oo. Strip ai · oa · ie · ee · or · ng · oo: There and back — beat your time! Gesture recall: nose-hum si-ng! + cuckoo oo-oo!

Sort — тетрадь стр.40 №2 (all vote first). Three columns: ng · oo долгий (moon) · oo короткий (book). Word up (ring · moon · book · song · zoo · foot) → read together → every child shows 1, 2 or 3 fingers → all write it in the right column.

Dictation — стр.40 №3 (short, story day). 3 words only, once each: king · moon · book → segment on fingers → write → Show me! Tricky strip №4: one · only — there and back.

Story taste — стр.41 «Король поёт» (1–2′). Chorus the 5 frames, finger under the words: A king. The king has corn… Ring, ring! The king sings! The draw-task (стр.41 №2) + «Кот и луна» (стр.42) go home. Colour the star (стр.40).

Sight words. Flash there · once: chorus, air-write — Once upon a time… (a story lesson word!).

💪 Support: sort with the word card in hand — place & copy. ⭐ Extension: 4th dictation word (song). No long written work — today is the fullest story lesson. 🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/) — Station 4/5, ✅ готов.
