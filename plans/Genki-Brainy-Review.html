Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will re-use the whole Brainy level with confidence — numbers 1–12 · How old are you? · What's the weather? · Good morning! · Do you like…? · I'm sorry! — in a joyful, celebratory review with a flagship team quiz.
  • Subsidiary. Song medley of the level; the ONE flagship team quiz with an animated reward (World Cup Soccer!); folder review worksheet (mixed stations); a sense of achievement.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; keep teams tiny & fair so every child answers; celebrate effort not winning; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘; end on a triumphant high.
By the end, pupils can…
  • answer any level question in the medley;
  • count 1–12 & say their age;
  • tell today's weather & the right greeting;
  • answer Do you like…? and say sorry kindly;
  • play a team quiz taking fair turns.

Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: (team quiz) "Whose turn? Take turns, one at a time?" → yes.
ICQ: "We cheer for EVERY team?" → yes.

Assumptions: children have met all 10 Brainy lessons. This is the ONE lesson with a scored team quiz + animated reward. Keep it light & inclusive. Near-zero English + L1 prompt. Non-readers. Group = 12 (3 teams of 4).

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Losing team gets upsetScore with the fun soccer/banana animation, keep it close, cheer every point; a reward sticker for all.
Fast children answer for everyoneNumbered turns in the team; each child gets their own question.
Too many topics at onceGroup the quiz by topic corners (numbers, weather, food/animals…), one at a time.
Over-excitement after ⚡🧘 settler after each ⚡; volume-control arm.
Transitions get chaoticTrain: Come with me! — one signal, one destination.
Classroom management — signals
"Class? — Yes!" — you: "Class!" → children stop & echo "Yes!"
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
"Speaky says…" — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
"Freeze!" / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — "5… 4… 3… 2… 1…" with fingers shown.
"Come with me!" 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Review = celebration of the whole level. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). The cumulative recycle opens the lesson (song medley + Q&A); the ONE scored team quiz with an animated reward lives here (never in normal lessons); the finale ends the level on a triumphant high + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song, "choose the greeting → move to your chair".
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up; Speaky greets each child.
  • Remember time — whole-level medley + cumulative Q&A chain.
  • Review quiz — flagship team quiz across all topics, with an animated reward (Review only).
  • Table time 🪑 — mixed review worksheet (stations), listen → do → say.
  • Reading time 🟢 — our S&S review big-book (Speaky).
  • Final game ⚡ — grand celebratory game, level ends on a high note.
  • Closing — Thank You! + Goodbye song, achievement check, reward stickers.
