🦝 Give Me Five! 1 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 9 / Intro L9 — May I borrow your…? (school things + politeness)
Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 28.09–04.10 · Занятие 9✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.9 · z·qu — алфавит закрыт! 🎉
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 8 school things and ask politely: “May I borrow your (glue)? — Of course! Here you are. — Thank you!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.9: z·qu — the LAST sounds: весь алфавит + qu пройден! Story «Як и жук»; recycle colours (May I borrow your RED pencil?) and numbers (two pencils, please!).
  • Personal. Politeness is the point — praise every Thank you / Here you are as loudly as correct vocabulary.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name stapler · glue · eraser · calculator · brush · tape · pencil sharpener · paint;
  • run the 4-line borrowing dialogue with real objects;
  • read and write zip, buzz, quiz — and celebrate: all 26 letters + qu are learnt!

Phonics

Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“May I borrow — забираю навсегда или верну?” → верну! (mime giving back)
“qu — q гуляет одна?” → no! q и u — неразлучные друзья: /кв/!

Assumptions: From занятия 1–8: colours (fresh — recycle), numbers, Do you like…?, all sounds s→y. School objects are physically in the classroom — use real ones, cards only as backup.

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Dialogue chain collapses to grabbingObjects stay on the table until the FULL chain is said; teacher shadows the first rounds.
«borrow» meaning unclearAct it: borrow Speaky's brush, use it, GIVE IT BACK with Thank you; CCQ верну/заберу.
stapler / sharpener sound-alikeContrast pair with gestures (chk-chk vs twist); slow-mo drill.
qu written as q alone«q без u не ходит» — chant qu-qu-quack while air-writing the pair.
End-of-alphabet excitement → rushing the storyStory is echo-read line by line; the celebration comes AFTER the last frame (big cheer + алфавит-парад).

Speed reading — стр.23 №1. Strip s→y — почти весь алфавит! Beat your time.

Meet z · qu. z — пчела: zzz! · qu — утка: кв-кв — quack! Spiky line: q и u — неразлучные друзья, вместе дают /кв/. Copy ×3.

Blend — стр.24 №1. Windows: /z/ /i/ /p/ — zip! /qu/ /i/ /z/ — quiz! … buzz, quilt, squid, yak.

Trace — стр.23 (desks). Прописи z · qu · zip · quiz; whisper.

Find the word — стр.24 №2. Hidden words in letter strings (a-zip-to · o-buzz-at · i-quiz-an) — read the string slowly together, every child circles the real word; partner check.

Dictation — стр.24 №3. Segment → write → Show me! Words: zip, buzz, quiz. Tricky strip do · no (do — из «Yes, I DO»!). Medal ⭐.

Story — стр.25 «Як и жук» + АЛФАВИТ-ПАРАД (settler → cheer). Echo-read: A big yak. A red bug!… Then the parade: flash all 26+qu cards in order — class voices each sound with its gesture — YOU KNOW THE WHOLE ALPHABET! CHEER! 🎉

Objects. Real things up one by one: What's this? — Glue! Chorus ×3: stapler (chk-chk!), glue, eraser, calculator, brush, tape, sharpener (twist!), paint. Flash-check out of order.

The chain. Model with Speaky: May I borrow your brush? — Of course! Here you are. — Thank you! — and Speaky GIVES IT BACK: Here you are! — Thank you! Back-chain the question; drill the 3 replies as a clap-ritual. ICQ: забираю насовсем? → нет, верну!

34–41′
Song & borrowing chain game
carpet circle

Sing «May I Borrow Your…?» passing objects on the beat.

▶ Chain borrow: every child holds ONE object. Simultaneous pairs (not one-by-one!): turn to your neighbour — May I borrow your […]? — Of course! Here you are. — Thank you! — objects swap; rotate the circle one step, go again. 4–5 rounds — every child asks AND answers every round.
