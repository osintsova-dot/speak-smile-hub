Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 10 food words and ask politely in a shop: “Can I have (a peach / some bread), please? — Here you are. — Thank you!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.17b · ou·oi — полноценная презентация по тетради; повтор прошлого материала, вплетённый в новую тему (см. Retrieval).
  • Personal. Лексика ТОЛЬКО в контексте структуры; чередуем стиры и сеттлеры; каждый ребёнок отвечает каждый раунд (хор, пары, show-me).
By the end, pupils can…
  • name the 10 foods;
  • ask the shop chunk Can I have…, please? and answer politely;
  • gap-fill ou/oi words and write the dictation.

Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Can I have… — я беру сам или вежливо прошу?” → прошу.
“Bread — можно сказать two breads?” → нет (не считаем).

Assumptions: U1 пройден. Овощи из з.8 (carrots, tomatoes, potatoes!) — уже знакомы: повтор вплетается сам. May I borrow (з.12) — родственная вежливая просьба. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).

Phonics Hero — ou·oi (b)
board → desks

Speed reading — the big strip. Board / Hub: ai · oa · ie · ee · or · ng · oo · ch · sh · th + yesterday's ou · oi. There and back — beat your time! Recall the calls: hands round the mouth ou! (ау!) · surprised sailor oi! (ой!) — all ×3.

Gap-fill — тетрадь стр.47 №2 «Допиши слово» (all vote first). Picture up (cloud · coin · mouth · soil) — say the word together, then every child calls the sound: «ау» or «ой»? → all write ou or oi into the gap: cl__d · c__n · m__th · s__l. RallyCoach: partner checks before the next word.

Dictation — стр.47 №3 (everyone writes). Now YOU write. Listen: out… word once → segment on fingers (/ou/ /t/) → write → Show me! → reveal on the board, self-check. Words: out, loud, coin, oil.

Tricky strip — стр.47 №4. your · she — read the strip for speed, there and back. + today's sight pair want · because — flash, chorus, air-write: 🦝 Speaky: I WANT some sweets… because they are yummy! (today's shop words, now in print).

Story — стр.48 «Королева и туча» (settler). Chorus the 5 frames, finger under the words: A queen. The queen looks out. A big cloud is loud! The queen is quick. Shh! The queen is proud. ⭐ Star task: find & circle every queen, circle how many. Colour the star «Я молодец!» (стр.47).

🎮 Reading-game option: read-race — ⚔ battle mode, level «Диграфы»: a word pops up, everyone reads it aloud, the fastest correct team races ahead. Swap it in for the speed-reading strip when the screen is free.

💪 Support: in the gap-fill give the digraph card to copy; in dictation write _ut. ⭐ Extension: a 5th dictation word by ear (cloud); in the story find out too.

✔ Assess: the dictation lines are your live check of ур.17 — note who writes ou/oi as one letter; re-drill at the next speed reading.
