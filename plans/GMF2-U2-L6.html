Week 26.10–01.11 · Lesson 16🏆 Flagship Team Quiz + reward~12 pupils · 3 teamsPupil's Book p.26 · AB pp.23, 25
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will have consolidated Unit 2 — 10 foods, a/an/some, numbers 10–50, How much is/are…? — and shown it in the flagship Team Quiz with reward game.
  • Subsidiary. PB Review p.26 (CD1 47–48) + «Well done!» song; spelling check (Learning to learn); traffic-light self-assessment before the L7 test.
  • Personal. Rotate the reward shooter fairly; feed the week's error harvest (a apple, THIRteen/THIRty, is/are) into quiz tiles.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name and spell unit food words;
  • use a/an/some + How much…? accurately under game pressure;
  • self-assess honestly with the traffic light.
