Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will have consolidated the whole Giant level — places, how-do-you-say, robot, Mr Octopus, time, summer & winter sports — and used the language joyfully in a flagship team quiz.
  • Subsidiary. A review-station carousel; the S&S big-book of favourites; a Genki graduate medal / certificate craft — celebrate finishing the whole preschool course.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; keep it a happy CELEBRATION, no test pressure; every child answers & shoots in a fast rotation (no queue); alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • answer Where do you live? — I live near the…!;
  • ask How do you say … in English?;
  • do robot commands & count Mr Octopus' arms;
  • tell the time What time is it? — It's … o'clock / half past…!;
  • say I like…! for summer & winter sports.

Classroom management — signals
"Class? — Yes!" — you: "Class!" → children stop & echo "Yes!"
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
"Speaky says…" — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
"Freeze!" / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — "5… 4… 3… 2… 1…" with fingers shown.
"Come with me!" 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages (Review shape)

Review lesson — consolidation & celebration. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Finale = the flagship quiz + Genki graduation + Thank You! song.

  • Opening + Warm up — door greeting, Hello + Disco Warm Up, Speaky handshake.
  • Remember time — the FULL cumulative Q&A of the whole Giant level as a fast quiz.
  • Review stations (carousel) — 4 topic corners the class travels through as a train; a quick task at each.
  • 🏆 Flagship quiz — Speaky's Basketball / Bowling (two teams): answer → take a shot → score; reward. Review-only.
  • Table time 🪑 — make a "Genki graduate" medal / certificate (mini-craft) & name your favourite topic.
  • Reading time 🟢 — a short favourite big-book.
  • Closing + graduation — celebrate finishing the course, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + medal.

L2 Now… Speaky's Basketball Quiz! Two teams! Answer, then SHOOT! Ready?

L1 Флагманская игра-награда (ТОЛЬКО на Review): 2 команды. Правильный ответ → бросок мяча в корзину (или сбить кегли — боулинг). Каждый ребёнок отвечает и бросает по очереди — никто не ждёт долго.

Speaky shows a card / asks a question from any Giant topic (Where do you live? How do you say…? What time is it? Do you like…?). The child answers → takes ONE basketball shot (or bowls to knock the pins). A basket = a point for the team. Numbered rotation so every child answers & shoots. The team chants the answer together. Both teams cheer; both win a reward at the end.
