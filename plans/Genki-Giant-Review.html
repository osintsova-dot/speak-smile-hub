Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will have recycled the whole Giant level with confidence — Where do you live? · How do you say…? · I am a robot! · Mr Octopus · What time is it? · Did you go …? — in a joyful medley + team quiz + celebration.
  • Subsidiary. Achievement check across all 8 lessons; the level’s flagship team quiz with animated rewards (World Cup Soccer! + Ski Game scoring); certificates.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; keep it celebratory, praise every attempt; every child answers every round (teams + chorus); alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • answer any Giant question in a quick round;
  • sing along to every Giant song;
  • play a team quiz taking turns fairly;
  • show their achievement grid is full;
  • celebrate finishing the Giant level!

ICQ: “In the quiz, do we shout out OR wait for our turn?” → wait for the team’s turn.

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Cumulative Q&A recycle lives at the start (Remember time); the final game is on today’s language.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives & shakes hands with each child (Hello! How are you?).
  • Remember time — cumulative review with A4 flashcards / Genki Karuta / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheet, a different type each lesson, always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Final game ⚡ — back to the carpet for a fast, fun game on today’s language, ending on a high note.
  • Closing — Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
