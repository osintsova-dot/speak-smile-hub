Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 10 holiday places and meet the past: “Where were you yesterday? — I was at the (circus)!”
  • Subsidiary. Sing the holiday song (CD4 11) + stickers chant; retrieve town places (U8) and Let's-go suggestions; first taste of was/were as a CHUNK (full grammar in L2); dream-holiday personalisation.
  • Personal. The past is a magic door — open it with wonder (“yesterday!”), keep it chunk-level today; summer is coming — ride that energy.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name the 10 holiday places;
  • answer “Where were you yesterday?” with the chunk “I was at the…”;
  • match place ↔ what you do there (Let's go… to see fish!).

Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“I was at the circus — today or YESTERDAY?” → yesterday (thumb back!).
“Where do we see fish — the castle or the aquarium?” → the aquarium!

Assumptions: Town-places logic (U8) transfers: place + function. was/were is brand new — chunk first, system in L2. Holiday dreams are high-energy fuel in April. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).
