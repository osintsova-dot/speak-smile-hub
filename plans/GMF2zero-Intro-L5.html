🦝 Give Me Five! 2 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 5 / Intro L5 — What's your favourite colour?
Pre-A1 young learners (~8–9 y.o., zero beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 14.09–20.09 · Занятие 5✨ NEW · 13 colours + favourite~10 pupils · pairs & 2 teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.5 · d·g·o
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 13 colours and say the favourite one: “What's your favourite colour? — It's blue. I like blue!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.5: sounds d·g·o + история «История про кота»; recycle numbers (count coloured things).
  • Personal. Colours in context of favourites and real objects; the metallic three (bronze·silver·gold) — как «медали», это круто для 8–9 лет.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name 13 colours incl. bronze · silver · gold;
  • ask & answer What's your favourite colour? — It's (red)!;
  • blend & write dog, dig, hot, top and read the cat story.

Structure
What's your favourite colour? — It's (blue). / I like (blue)! · How many (blue) things? — Three!
Form
favourite = самый любимый (ОДИН!); It's = It is. Числа + цвета вместе: three blue pencils.
Pron. watch
purple /ˈpɜːpl/ two dark beats; orange /ˈɒrɪndʒ/; gold clean /əʊ/.
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.5 (тетрадь стр.12–14): звуки d·g·o + жесты · блендинг (dog, red, dig, hot, top, dot) · «обведи слово» · диктант · история «История про кота» · tricky to, you.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Favourite — все цвета или ОДИН самый-самый?” → один.
“Red — a colour or a number?” → colour!

Assumptions: Занятия 1–4 done. Часть цветов дети узнают — но с нуля шлифуем произношение и НОВОЕ: bronze/silver/gold + вопрос favourite.

9–24′
Phonics Hero ур.5 — d·g·o
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.12 №1. Strip s·a·t·i·p·n·c·k·e·h·r·m — twelve sounds! There and back; boys/girls/whisper/LOUD.

Meet d · g · o. d — барабаним: d-d-d! · g — глоток воды: g-g-g! · o — губы бубликом, щёлк выключателем: o-o! Copy ×3, mixed flash.

Blend — стр.13 №1. Windows: /d/ /o/ /g/ — dog! … red, dig, hot, top, dot — air-blend, pictures for meaning.

Trace — стр.12 (desks). Прописи d · g · o · dog · got; whisper the sounds. Then «обведи слово» — стр.13 №2 (dog/dot/dig, map/mop/man, cot/cat/can, ten/tan/tin): read all three together, every child circles, partner check.

Dictation — стр.13 №3. Segment on fingers → write → Show me! Words: dog, dig, hot, top. Tricky strip to · you. Medal ⭐.

Story — стр.14 «История про кота» (settler). Echo-read the 5 frames, finger under the words: Sam has a cat. The cat sat in a hat… ⭐ считаем: how many times is cat? — count in English! (5). And the hat — What colour is the hat? Colour it your FAVOURITE colour! — сегодняшняя тема прямо в истории.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «c k e h r m d» + история on screen. ◆ Support: blend only dog, hot. ▲ Extension: word 5 by ear (dot). ✔ Assess: Show-me dictation; story star-count = number check too.

Presentation · 13 colours

Colour cards. Flash 10 base colours briskly (many known — pace fast): chorus ×2 each. Then the MEDALS: bronze… silver… GOLD! — Olympic podium gesture, 8–9-летки обожают.

The favourite question. Back-chain: colour? → favourite colour? → What's your favourite colour? 🦝 Speaky: My favourite colour is… PURPLE! (конечно 💜). Ask 3–4 children: It's blue! I like blue!

▶ Colour touch: Touch something GREEN! — all dash and touch; 5 fast rounds, finish with Freeze!
