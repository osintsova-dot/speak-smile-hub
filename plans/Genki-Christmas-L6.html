🎅 GenkiEnglish Preschool · Christmas · Lesson 6 (L32) — What would you like for Christmas? — robot, doll, bear, bike, book; I’d like a…
Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
Christmas block · Lesson 6 of 7✨ presents · What would you like…? I’d like a…12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to name 5 present toys (robot, doll, bear, bike, book) and ask/answer What would you like for Christmas? — I’d like a… robot! Thank you, Santa!
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time recycle of presents (L27) & colours (a red bike); the S&S big-book; a card-to-Santa craft (draw/stick the wish & say it).
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; the request always in the exchange with Santa/partner; every child asks AND answers every round (Mix-Pair-Share, no queue); ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name 5 present toys;
  • answer What would you like? — I’d like a bear!;
  • ask a partner the question;
  • say Thank you, Santa!;
  • make a card to Santa & say the wish.

Pron. watch
I’d like = “I would like” contracted, keep it soft; bear /beə/ (not “beer”); keep Thank you, Santa! warm & grateful.

This is the language children love most — personalised wishes. Non-readers/non-writers (no phonics). Attention 5–7 min → ⚡/🧘 rhythm. Group = 12.

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Festive touch: Speaky wears a Santa hat (today he can BE Santa!) & the bus-box gets a New Year hat. Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the festive bus-box & shakes hands.
  • Remember time — cumulative review (presents + colours) with A4 flashcards / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box (Santa’s sack), the wish song, board app, active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder / craft, a different type each lesson; today a card to Santa (draw/stick the wish), always language-linked (choose → make → say).
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
