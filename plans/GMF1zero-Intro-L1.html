Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks · FIRST LESSON EVER
Week 03.09–06.09 · Занятие 1✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.1 · s·a·t
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to follow 7 classroom commands (TPR) and introduce themselves with “What's your name? — My name is…”.
  • Subsidiary. First phonics lesson: sounds s·a·t (звуки, не буквы!) → blend the very first word sat; set the class routines (carpet spots, signals, Speaky ritual).
  • Personal. Model EVERYTHING — zero English assumed; keep instructions to 3–4 words + gesture; smile a lot: today decides how children feel about English.
By the end, pupils can…
  • do stand up / sit down / hands up / hands down / bow / clap / cheer on command;
  • say “My name is […]” when asked;
  • voice s·a·t and blend at → sat — the first read word!

Structure
What's your name? — My name is […]. · Nice to meet you! · Rock, paper, scissors — 1, 2, 3!
Form
What + 's (is) + your name? Answer = name only is OK at first; build to “My name is…”.
Pron. watch
“name” /eɪ/ not /а/; whole-phrase rhythm WHAT's your NAME? (clap the two beats).
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.1 (тетрадь стр.3–4): звуки s /с/ · a /э/ · t /т/ + жесты · прописи · первый блендинг at, sat · «найди буквы» · tricky strip the, a.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Do we say the letter name «эс» — or the sound?” → the sound: sss!
“When I ask What's your name? — do you say Hello or your name?” → your name.

Assumptions: NOTHING is assumed — this is the children's first English lesson ever. No routines, no phonics, no classroom English yet: every ritual (carpet spots, signals, Speaky) is being set today. Use Russian sparingly for safety/logistics only. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
First-day shyness / tearsChorus first, individual later; let a shy child answer holding Speaky; praise every attempt.
Children don't understand instructionsDemo everything with your body + Speaky; 3–4-word instructions; same phrases every time.
They name letters («эс, а, тэ»)Always model the SOUND with the gesture; CCQ “sound or name?” each round.
Rock-paper-scissors argumentsPre-teach “I win! / Oh, no! / Good game!”; losers cheer the winner — that's the game culture.
Over-excitement after DiscoTeach “Freeze!” in the very first stage and use it playfully 2–3 times.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → pupils stop & echo “Yes!”
“WiFi” 🖐 — say “WiFi” + raise a spread hand → pupils silently mirror & tune in.
Volume control — raise/lower your arm = louder/quieter → settler.
“Give me five” — eyes · ears · mouth · hands · feet.
Countdown — “Finishing in 5…4…3…2…1… pencils down.”
“Freeze!” — statue, mid-movement reset.

Phonics Hero ур.1 — s·a·t
board → desks

The big secret (Spiky strip, стр.3). 🦝 Speaky: мы учим не названия букв, а звуки — s это «с-с-с», не «эс». Так слова складываются сами!

Meet s · a · t (+ жесты). s — змейка рукой: sss! · a — муравей ползёт по руке: a-a-a! · t — смотрим теннис влево-вправо: t-t-t! All copy sound + gesture ×3; flash cards out of order — children voice them.

Trace — стр.3 (desks, settle). Обведи по точкам, стрелки показывают куда; start at the yellow dot: s · a · t · at · sat — whisper each sound as you write.

FIRST WORD — blend! Sound windows on the board: /a/ /t/ — at! /s/ /a/ /t/ — sat! Every child slides a “blend finger” in the air. Celebrate loudly: You can READ! Cheer! 🎉

Find the letters — стр.4. Найди и обведи все s, a, t среди других букв — race format: Ready, steady… go! Partner check. Tricky strip the · a: flash, chorus ×3 — «хитрые слова, просто запоминаем». Colour the medal ⭐.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «s a t i p n», Station «Звуки»: cards on the projector, children voice each. Offline: тетрадь + рукописные карточки. ◆ Support: trace with finger first. ▲ Extension: write sat on the spare line. ✔ Assess: who says letter names instead of sounds — recast with the gesture.

Model with Speaky. What's your name? — 🦝 My name is Speaky! Nice to meet you! + handshake. Back-chain the question: name? → your name? → What's your name? Chorus ×3.
