Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will have consolidated the whole High level — What sports do you play? · When is your birthday? · How did you get here? · What colour is it? · What's your favourite colour / flavour / subject? — and celebrated a year of Genki English.
  • Subsidiary. Whole-year Q&A recycle; the ONE flagship team quiz with animated rewards (allowed only at Review); a keepsake “Genki Year” certificate.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; celebration tone — everyone succeeds; every child speaks/sings every round; teams so all answer together, not one-by-one; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • answer any High question What sports do you play? / When is your birthday? / How did you get here?;
  • say their favourites colour · flavour · subject;
  • sing the High song medley with mimes;
  • receive their Genki-Year certificate proudly.

Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: (World Cup Soccer) “The WHOLE team answers together — one child or all?” → all.
ICQ: “Right answer → we move the ball / grow a banana?” → yes, a reward each time.

Assumptions: children have finished all 8 High lessons. Review = celebration, not testing — keep it warm & winnable. Non-readers → picture cards + board app. Group = 12 (two teams of 6).

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Quiz = one child answers, rest watchWHOLE team answers chorally each turn; rotate who touches the board. PIES — everyone speaks.
Losing team upsetBoth teams win a prize; celebrate effort; keep scores close & the mood light.
Party over-excitementFreeze! statue + volume-control arm; countdown to settle.
Song medley too longOne chorus per song, keep the mimes big & the pace up.
Certificate rushName each child; a calm proud moment; sticker on the folder.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages (Review = celebration)

Practice-proven skeleton — Review keeps the frame but turns it into a party. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). The cumulative recycle lives at the start; the flagship team quiz with animated rewards (World Cup Soccer / Banana Tree / Ski Game) lives ONLY here.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), move to your chair.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives & shakes hands (Hello! How are you?).
  • Remember time — cumulative whole-year Q&A + a grab game.
  • Review carousel — the High song medley with mimes + board app.
  • Flagship team quiz ⚡ — World Cup Soccer! with Banana Tree scoring (reward animations).
  • Table time 🪑 — Genki-Year certificate / passport mini-craft (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🟢 — best-of Speaky big-book of the year.
  • Final game ⚡ — a joyful whole-group celebration round on a high note.
  • Closing — certificate ceremony + Thank You! + Goodbye song, sticker on the folder.
