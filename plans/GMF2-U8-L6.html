Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will have consolidated Unit 8 — 10 places, Is-there/Are-there-any, directions — and shown it in the flagship Team Quiz with reward game.
  • Subsidiary. PB Review p.90 map work; street-writing (AB format); spelling; traffic light.
  • Personal. Error harvest (any-ticket, left/right, chemist's /k/) into tiles; shooter rotation; map tiles use OUR floor town.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name/spell unit places;
  • run flip-questions + directions under game pressure;
  • self-assess honestly.
