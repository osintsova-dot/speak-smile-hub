🦝 GenkiEnglish Preschool · High · Lesson 4 — How did you get here?
Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
High level · Lesson 4 of 9 · final year✨ I came by… · 8 vehicles12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to name 8 vehicles (aeroplane, car, train, rocket, helicopter, boat, bus, bike) and answer How did you get here? — I came by car!.
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time recycle of months/birthday & sports; the S&S big-book; a colour-&-craft folder sheet (my vehicle).
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; transport always in context (I came by bus!); every child moves/says every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name 8 vehicles with a movement
  • answer How did you get here? — I came by train!
  • run & touch the right transport card
  • move like a vehicle in a mingle
  • colour & tell their vehicle.

Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: "Do we MOVE or watch?" → move.
ICQ: "Say "I came by…" when you touch?" → yes.

Assumptions: children have a year of routine → meaning from movement, toy vehicles, music & the L1 prompt. The chunk I came by… (past) is learnt whole, not analysed. Non-readers/non-writers. Attention 5–7 min. Group = 12.

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
helicopter/aeroplane hard to sayPair with a clear movement; say word + move together, 3×; the song carries it.
Moving around → collisionsSet the space, move on the spot first; Freeze! + volume arm.
A child names no vehicleOffer two: Car or bus? point is fine, then echo the chunk.
Past form confuses adults, not kidsKeep I came by… as one lump; no grammar talk.
Over-excitement🧘 settler after each ⚡; train between zones.
Classroom management — signals
"Class? — Yes!" — you: "Class!" → children stop & echo "Yes!"
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
"Speaky says…" — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
"Freeze!" / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — "5… 4… 3… 2… 1…" with fingers shown.
"Come with me!" 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song, "choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair".
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the bus-box & shakes hands with each child.
  • Remember time — cumulative review (thumb-up/down Q&A like a game) with A4 flashcards / memory / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, one active game.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheets, a different type each lesson (draw & tell / match & stick / order / colour by command / listen & tick / craft), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🟢 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
