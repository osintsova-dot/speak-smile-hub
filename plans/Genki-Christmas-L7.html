Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
Christmas block · Lesson 7 of 7✨ Happy New Year! · last lesson before the holidays · PARTY12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to say Happy New Year! with a wave/cheer and joyfully re-use the whole Christmas block (words · colours · shapes · snowman · wishes) in a party of songs & games.
  • Subsidiary. Whole-block review woven into a New Year celebration; the S&S big-book; a New Year garland craft (make & name the pictures).
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; the last lesson before the break — keep it a happy party, not a test; every child sings/moves/answers every round; alternate ⚡/🧘; end the term on a warm high.
By the end, pupils can…
  • say Happy New Year! with a cheer;
  • sing the whole Christmas-block set;
  • name Christmas words, colours, shapes & wishes in fast play;
  • make a New Year garland & name the pictures;
  • wish everyone Happy New Year! at the door.

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “3… 2… 1… Happy New Year!” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Festive touch: Speaky wears a party hat & the bus-box gets a New Year hat. This is the block finale — a party: the review is the celebration itself. Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the party bus-box & shakes hands.
  • Remember time — fast whole-block review carousel (words · colours · shapes · snowman · wishes) with A4 flashcards / app.
  • Focus time — the New Year moment (countdown + song) & a party song medley on the board, active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder / craft, a different type each lesson; today a New Year garland, always language-linked (make → hang → name).
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the whole-block Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
