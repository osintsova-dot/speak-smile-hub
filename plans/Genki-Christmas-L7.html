Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to celebrate the New Year in English — Happy New Year! · fireworks · count down · Goodbye! — inside Ten, nine… Happy New Year! See you next year!
  • Subsidiary. Party review of the WHOLE Christmas block (L1–L6) — this is the block finale, so the Remember-time is a full Christmas medley & showcase. New today: New-Year words & the countdown. Folder task: put-in-order.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; keep it a real party but held with signals; every child counts, jumps & sings every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘; warm send-off to the holidays.
By the end, pupils can…
  • say Happy New Year! with a cheer;
  • count down 10, 9, 8… Happy New Year!;
  • name fireworks & wave Goodbye! See you next year!;
  • join the Happy New Year song;
  • show off Christmas words from the whole block.

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “10… 9… 8…” with fingers shown (today it's the theme!).
“Come with me!” 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). This is the Christmas-block finale: the Remember-time is a full-block party review, and a team celebration game (Ski Game) is used just this once for a festive showcase.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives from the bus-box & shakes hands with each child (Happy New Year! How are you?).
  • Remember time — full Christmas-block review: A4 flashcards medley + Ski Game showcase on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, New-Year song, board app, active Genki games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheet, a different type each lesson (this lesson: put-in-order the countdown), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🟢 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Final game ⚡ — back to the carpet for a fast, fun game on today's language, so the block ends on a high note.
  • Closing — Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker, warm send-off to the holidays.
