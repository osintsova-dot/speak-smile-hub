Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 14.09–20.09 · Занятие 4✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.4 · h·r·m
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to use pronouns with be in context: “I am hungry. You are great. He / She is happy. We are happy!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.4: sounds h·r·m, sort by first sound, dictation; recycle feelings (зан.2) + can-actions (зан.3) inside the new structure (“He can fly — he is happy!”).
  • Personal. Teach pronouns ONLY through gestures + real people in the room — no grammar talk, no tables.
By the end, pupils can…
  • point and say I / you / he / she / we / they / everybody with the right gesture;
  • build “He is hungry / She is happy / We are happy!” about real people and pictures;
  • read and write hat, red, ham, map; sort words by h / r / m.

Pron. watch

Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.4 (тетрадь стр.10–11): h /х-выдох/ · r /р/ · m /м/ · blending hat, hen, rat, red, ham, map · сортировка по первому звуку · диктант hat, red, ham, map · tricky me.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
Point at a boy: “he or she?” → he! At a girl? → she! At the whole class (circle arms)? → we!
“You are great — am I talking about me or about YOU?” → about you (point).

Assumptions: From занятия 1–3: greetings, feelings (happy/sad/tired/hungry/thirsty), actions + can, sounds s→e, CVC blending, dictation routine. he/she is genuinely new — Russian он/она helps, use it once then stay in English.

Phonics Hero ур.4 — h·r·m
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.10 №1. Strip s·a·t·i·p·n·c·k·e — nine sounds! There and back, beat your time.

Meet h · r · m. h — руку на грудь, дышим как собачка: h-h-h! · r — трясём тряпочку, рычим: rrr! · m — гладим животик, вкусно: mmm! Copy ×3, mixed flash.

Blend — стр.11 №1. Sound windows: /h/ /a/ /t/ — hat! /r/ /e/ /d/ — red! … hen, rat, ham, map — air-blend, picture for meaning.

Trace — стр.10 (desks). Прописи h · r · m · hat · ram — whisper as you write.

Sort — стр.11 №2 (all vote). Word up (hat, rat, map, hen, mat, red) → read together → every child shows the first sound with its GESTURE (dog-breath / growl / yummy) → write in the right column. Partner check (RallyCoach).

Dictation — стр.11 №3. Segment on fingers → write → Show me! Words: hat, red, ham, map. Tricky strip me (+ quick re-flash of said, he — and notice: he is today's grammar word!). Medal ⭐.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «c k e h r m d»: «Звуки» + «Блендинг». ◆ Support: sort only 3 words. ▲ Extension: extra word by ear (hen). ✔ Assess: gesture-vote in the sort shows every child's first-sound hearing.
