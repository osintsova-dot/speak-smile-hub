🎄 GenkiEnglish Preschool · Christmas · Lesson 3 — What Colour is Christmas?
Genki preschoolers (~3.5–5 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
Christmas block · Lesson 3 of 7✨ Christmas colours (review) + What colour…?12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to say the Christmas colours — red · white · green · gold · blue · purple · silver — inside the question What colour is Christmas? — It's red!
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time recycle of all earlier topics + Christmas words so far (L1–L2), applied to NEW colour language. New vocab lives ONLY in context. Folder task: colour by command.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; model every line first; every child names/points every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘; keep the festive magic high.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name 7 Christmas colours;
  • answer What colour is Christmas? — It's gold!;
  • point to / jump to a called colour;
  • join the What Colour is Christmas? song;
  • colour an ornament on command.

TPR gesture
each colour = touch something that colour + a matching mime (gold sparkle fingers · silver shimmer · green tree arms · white snow flutter · red Santa hat pat).
Pron. watch
keep What colour is Christmas? as one sing-song chunk; bright It's ___!. Don't drill single colours — pointing & singing carry it.
Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: “I call gold — you touch/jump to GOLD only?” → yes.
ICQ: (worksheet) “Colour the bell the colour I SAY?” → yes.

Assumptions: children had L1–L2 Christmas words + the autumn block; some colours were met earlier (red/blue/green). Gold/silver/purple may be new. Near-zero English + L1 prompt. Non-readers. Group = 12.

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Cumulative Q&A recycle lives at the start (Remember time); the final game is on today's Christmas language.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives from the bus-box & shakes hands with each child (Merry Christmas! How are you?).
  • Remember time — cumulative review with A4 flashcards / Criss Cross Game / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, active Genki games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheet, a different type each lesson (this lesson: colour by command), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🟢 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Final game ⚡ — back to the carpet for a fast, fun game on today's language, so the lesson ends on a high note.
  • Closing — Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
