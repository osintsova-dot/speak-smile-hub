Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 6 ball actions (hit · jump · kick · bounce · throw · catch) and describe: “He throws the ball. / She catches the ball.”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.16a · ch·sh·th — полный этап по тетради; лексика и структура юнита вплетены в новый материал.
  • Personal. Новый набор + структура сразу вместе (в контексте); больше движения — этот урок самый «плотный» в юните.
By the end, pupils can…
  • do & say the 6 actions;
  • describe: She bounces the ball — it's basketball!;
  • meet ch·sh·th and blend chip · ship.
Target language
Lexis

Structure
He (throws) the ball. / She (catches) the ball. · game chunk: She bounces the ball — it's basketball!
Form
he/she + verb+s: throws, kicks, catches (лишний слог!), bounces. «-s принадлежит he/she».
Pron. watch
catches /ɪz/ — два слога; throw /θrəʊ/ язычок; hit короткое /ɪ/ (не «heat»).
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.16a · ch·sh·th (тетрадь стр.43) — этап «Phonics Hero — ch·sh·th (a)»: полный ход в Procedure ниже.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“catches — сколько слогов?” → два! /ˈкætʃɪz/.
“He throw или He throws?” → throws (-s-щелчок!).

Assumptions: L1–L3: спорты, do-вопрос, -s замечен в истории. Сегодня -s тренируем на 6 новых глаголах. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → pupils stop & echo “Yes!”
“WiFi” 🖐 — say “WiFi” + raise a spread hand → pupils silently mirror & tune in.
Volume control — raise/lower your arm = louder/quieter → settler.
“Give me five” — eyes · ears · mouth · hands · feet.
Countdown — “Finishing in 5…4…3…2…1… pencils down.”

Phonics Hero — ch·sh·th (a)
board → desks

Speed reading (тетрадь стр.43 №1). Strip ai · oa · ie · ee · or · ng · oo: There and back — fast!

Meet ch · sh · th (стр.43 №2). The train ch — ch-ch-ch! · the quiet-finger sh — shhh! · the clown th — «язычок между зубами». Tie-in to today's actions: CAtch — catch the ball! THrow — th! All copy each sound + gesture; quick show-me: say chair / shop / three / chip — all do the matching gesture.

Blend — Hub Station 3 / стр.44 №1 (all together). Sound windows: /ch/ /i/ /p/… chip! /sh/ /i/ /p/… ship! Then chin, shop, fish, moth — picture for meaning; contrast: chip… ship — same? No!

Trace (стр.43 №3, desks — quick). By the arrows: ch · sh · th · chip · ship — whisper as you write (finish lines at home; today's MAIN aim is actions + he/she + -s).

Sight words. Flash like · have (whole-word, not sounded): chorus, air-write.

💪 Support: gesture first, then sound; blend only chip·ship. ⭐ Extension: write fish on the spare line. 🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/) — набор ch·sh·th, ✅ готов; offline: тетрадь стр.44 №1.
