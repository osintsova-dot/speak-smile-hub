🦝 Give Me Five! 1 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 7 / Intro L7 — Do you like food? (vegetables)
Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 21.09–27.09 · Занятие 7✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.7 · b·j·v + история про пса
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 8 vegetables and ask & answer “Do you like (carrots)? — Yes, I do! / No, I don't!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.7: sounds b·j·v + the b/d tummy trick, story «История про пса»; recycle numbers (How many carrots?) and feelings (I'm hungry!) inside the food topic.
  • Personal. Honour real dislikes — “No, I don't!” is a WIN, not rudeness; model both answers with equal joy.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name onions · peas · carrots · beans · mushrooms · potatoes · pumpkin · tomatoes;
  • answer “Do you like…?” truthfully with Yes, I do! / No, I don't!;
  • read and write bug, bed, jam, van; tell b from d by the tummy trick.

Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“No, I don't — is it rude?” → No! It's honest. Both answers get a thumbs-up.
“Do you like peas? — can I answer «potato»?” → no — Yes, I do! or No, I don't!

Assumptions: From занятия 1–6: greetings, feelings (hungry!), numbers, How many?, age; sounds s→f + CVC routine. Vegetables partly known from home in Russian — pictures carry the meaning.

Phonics Hero ур.7 — b·j·v
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.17 №1. Strip s→f (18 sounds!). There and back; whisper → LOUD.

Meet b · j · v. b — отбиваем мяч: b-b! · j — трясём желе: j-j-j! · v — мотор фургона: vvv! Spiky trick: b и d — не путай! У b животик справа (кулачки-«bed»). Copy ×3, mixed flash.

Blend — стр.18 №1. Windows: /b/ /u/ /s/ — bus! /j/ /a/ /m/ — jam! … bug, bed, jet, van.

Trace — стр.17 (desks). Прописи b · j · v · bus · jam; whisper the sounds. Then «обведи слово» — стр.18 №2 (bug/bus/bun, bad/bed/bud, jet/jam/jab, van/vet/vat) — all read, all circle, partner check.

Dictation — стр.18 №3. Segment → write → Show me! Words: bug, bed, jam, van — watch the b-тummies! Tricky strip are · was (are — из «How old ARE you?»!). Medal ⭐.

Story — стр.19 «История про пса» (settler). Echo-read 5 frames, finger under words: Sam has a big dog… ⭐ draw-task goes home. Cheer!

Round 1 (4 cards). What's this? — Carrots! Chorus ×3 each: onions (cry!), peas (tiny fingers), carrots (crunch!), beans. Quick flash-check out of order.

Round 2 (4 more). mushrooms (umbrella hands), potatoes, pumpkin (big belly), tomatoes. Flash-check all 8.

Structure. Model with Speaky: Do you like onions, Speaky? — Nooo! No, I don't! (dramatic) Do you like jam? — YES, I DO! Chant both answers with claps. ICQ: «Нравится» — can I say just «yes»? → Yes, I do!
