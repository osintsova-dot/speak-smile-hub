Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 10 winter-fun actions and say what WE are doing: “We're (skating) on the ice! / We're (making a snowman)!”
  • Subsidiary. Sing the frozen-lake song (CD2 38) + stickers chant (CD2 40); post-holiday retrieval — Present Continuous I/he/she from U4 warmed up and extended to WE; holiday small-talk in English; winter theme = наша родная тема в Норильске!
  • Personal. First lesson after the break: generous retrieval, lower the bar, celebrate everything — confidence first, accuracy second.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name the 10 winter actions;
  • say “We're skating / We're drinking hot chocolate” about a picture and about the pair;
  • read the action phrases and match to pictures.

Structure

Form
we/they + 're (are) + verb-ing. The whole continuous family on one board: I'm / He's / She's / We're / They're.
Pron. watch
're almost invisible: We're /wɪə/ links into the verb; chocolate 2 beats /ˈtʃɒklət/; listen — silent t!
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“We're skating — one person or together?” → together, we!
“Are they doing it now or yesterday?” → now!

Assumptions: Two weeks of holidays = rust on everything; U4 continuous comes back fast with mime support. Winter actions map directly onto the children's real holiday — personalisation is easy fuel.

Presentation · 10 winter actions
carpet → board

Flashcards + mime. What are they doing? They're… skating! Choral ×3 + on-the-spot mime each. Long chunks clapped: take-a-PHO-to, lis-ten-to-MU-sic.

The WE step. Join hands gesture: Together — WE'RE skating! Class mimes in pairs, chorusing: We're dancing! We're waving! Family board: I'm / He's / She's / We're / They're.

▶ Take Off–Touch Down: Stand up if you did it in the holidays… making a snowman! …skating! — instant personalisation.

💪 Support: mime + verb word enough. ⭐ Extension: add the place: We're skating on the ice!
