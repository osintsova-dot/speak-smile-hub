Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will have consolidated all Unit 4 language — the food words (meat, eggs, fish, chips, rice, cake, salad, chicken, fruit, ice cream + pizza, soup, sandwiches, pasta) and the fruit (mango, grape, peach, cherry, lemon, coconut, apple, banana, orange), plus “I like / I don't like / I love (chicken)” and “Do you like (grapes)? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.” — and can use it confidently in the review tasks and the quiz.
  • Subsidiary. Phonics — Phonics Hero ур.23 (b): Magic E финал — speed columns a-e/i-e/o-e/u-e, final dictation (cake, bike, nose, cube…) + sentence dictation, star «Я знаю Magic E!»; tricky words right, goes; self-assessment (My progress traffic light, AB p. 45 ex. 3); listening for detail (PB p. 48 ex. 1, CD2 34).
  • Personal. Run the flagship quiz by PIES: every child writes/shows every answer (Showdown), the responder is picked by Numbered Heads — no “captains answer everything”; keep the reward moment short so language time wins.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name all the Unit 4 food & fruit words without support;
  • say “I like / I don't like / I love (salad)” about themselves;
  • ask & answer “Do you like (grapes)? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.”;
  • read all four Magic-E columns at speed and write the final dictation (words + a sentence);
  • self-assess with the traffic light — ready for the Unit 4 test.

Structure
I like / I don't like / I love (chicken). · Do you like (grapes)? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
Form
like / love / don't like + noun · Do + you + like + noun? → short answers Yes, I do. / No, I don't. (not “Yes, I like”).
Pron. watch
degree scale like → love (heavier stress on love); clear /dʒ/ in juice, jam, jelly; contracted don't /dəʊnt/; long /aɪ/ in rain, tail.
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.23 (b) — Magic E финал: speed columns a-e/i-e/o-e/u-e · final dictation cake, bike, nose, cube, time, home · sentence dictation I like my bike. / The cake is safe. · star «Я знаю Magic E!» (тетрадь стр.65). Tricky words: right, goes.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
Show ice cream + a big happy face: Do I LIKE it or LOVE it? Which is bigger? → love → “I love ice cream.”
Thumbs: Do you like fish? Thumb up = “Yes, I do.” Thumb down = “No, I don't.” (never “Yes, I like”).
Quiz rule ICQ: Who writes the answer — one child or EVERYBODY? → everybody, on their own board!

Assumptions: all Unit 4 food & fruit language met in L1–L5; the “j” sound was taught in L5 and the whole Magic-E block (Phonics Hero ур.19–23, стр.53–64) is done — today is the Magic-E final with its star. Teams of 4 with numbered members (1–4) are routine. The flagship quiz with reward runs only on Review day — children know it's special. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).

17–24′
Review in motion
📺 open space · pairs

▶ Taste Survey — Mix–Pair–Share (everyone talks at once, nobody waits). Music plays → children mix; Freeze! Pair up! → each child asks the nearest partner about their food card: Do you like chicken? — Yes, I do! / No, I don't. New music → new partner, new card. 4–5 rounds — the WHOLE class is speaking every round.

▶ Like / Don't-like / Love Corners (all move). Call a food — Ice cream! — children run to a corner (💛 LOVE · 🙂 LIKE · 🙁 DON'T LIKE) and say the full sentence: I love ice cream! Mix foods across 5–6 rounds; recycles like/don't like/love with movement. Reset: Class? — Yes!

Support ▸ weaker pupils keep their food picture card in hand + answer with the thumb gesture and one word (echo the chunk).   Extension ▸ stronger pupils add a reason-ish extra — No, I don't. I like fruit! — a two-part answer, not one word.

33–51′
🏆 FLAGSHIP QUIZ with reward
📺 teams · projector

Set-up (2 min). 3 teams of 4, numbered heads 1–4, one mini-whiteboard EACH. Quiz grid on screen — categories: Food & fruit 🍕 · Like / don't like / love 💛 · Do you like…? 👍👎 · Sounds & spelling 🔤. Rules + ICQ: Who writes? — Everybody! When do you show? — On “Showdown!”

Round flow (Kagan Showdown + Numbered Heads — all answer every question). Question up (e.g. picture half-hidden: What is it? · degree: Big smile — do I LIKE or LOVE it? · survey: Do you like grapes? Yes I do or No I don't? · sound: Write the word — s-n-ai-l! · read an “ai” / “j” word). Every child writes/draws → Showdown! → all boards up → call a number: Number 3! — that child of the playing team answers aloud in a full sentence.

Predict (thinking). On the half-hidden food picture, before revealing: Look — just a corner! What do you THINK it is? Write your guess! Then reveal — Were you right? Turns each round into a little guess-and-check.

Reward shot. Correct = the answering child takes the shot: animated football penalty on screen — a GOAL = team points, class counts them in English: One, two, three… GOAL! (No projector → paper goal + soft ball, ≈ Mido №91 Bowling for Animal reskin.) Rotate teams; over ~10–12 questions every child shoots at least once.

Support ▸ food word-box / picture bank stays visible on a side card so weaker writers copy, not freeze; accept one correct word from them.   Extension ▸ when a strong pupil is “Number”, require the full sentence — It's ice cream. I love ice cream! — not just the noun.
🔨 Flagship interactive · projector · BUILD · “Unit 4 Team Quiz + Reward”  🔨 BUILD
Screen 1 — Grid: 4×3 board of numbered tiles under the 4 category headers (Food & fruit 🍕 / Like·don't·love 💛 / Do you like…? 👍👎 / Sounds & spelling 🔤). Tap a tile → it flips to the question (picture / half-hidden food / audio word / “ai”/“j” word / degree prompt). Used tiles grey out.
Screen 2 — Answer: big “Show your boards!” + a 5-second timer bar; tap ✔ / ✗ to score; a full-sentence model appears for choral echo.
Screen 3 — Reward mini-game: football penalty — child “kicks”, goalie dives, animated GOAL (or a bowling reskin). One shot = +points.
Screen 4 — Scoreboard: 3 team bars, huge numbers readable from the back row, plus a “Teamwork ⭐” toggle for best cooperation.
Mechanic: pick tile → question → everyone writes → Showdown → Numbered Head answers → score → reward shot → rotate team. ~10–12 tiles ≈ 15 min.
Target language: all Unit 4 — What's this? / I like / don't like / love… / Do you like…? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't. + read an “ai” / “j” word.
Teacher: host only — YOU tap tiles & call the number so no captain dominates. Spec is a self-contained HTML slide (S&S purple #6C3FC5 / honey #F5C842). Offline reserve: printed grid + paper goal + soft ball works identically.

Finish with totals + teamwork star: Give me five, champions!

🎮 Ready-made engine for this quiz: food-quiz — the S&S bowling/basketball quiz on this unit, 1–4 teams: a picture question on screen → the team answers chorally — Do you like bread? → Yes, I do! → one child makes the throw on screen; the score counts itself.
