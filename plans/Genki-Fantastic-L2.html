🦝 GenkiEnglish Preschool · Fantastic · Lesson 50 — Can you speak English?
Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
Fantastic level · Lesson 2 of 6✨ 5 languages · Can you speak…?12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to name 5 languages (English, French, Chinese, Russian, Japanese) and ask & answer Can you speak English? — Yes, I can! / No, I can’t.
  • Subsidiary. Recycle the fruit market (What would you like?) & Hello in different languages; the S&S big-book; a flag match & colour sheet at Table time.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; language always in context (Can you speak…? with a friend, not a bare list); everyone answers every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name 5 languages;
  • ask Can you speak Russian?;
  • answer Yes, I can! / No, I can’t.;
  • say hello in each language;
  • match & colour the flags.

Chunks

TPR gesture
hand to mouth for speak; thumbs-up Yes, I can! / crossed hands No, I can’t.; wave hello per country.
Pron. watch
can’t vs can — stress the ’t; Chinese /tʃaɪˈniːz/; Japanese 3 beats.
Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: “Yes = thumbs up, No = …?” → crossed hands.
ICQ: “Do we ASK a friend or shout?” → ask a friend.

Assumptions: children finished the fruit market (Lesson 49) but are near-zero English for languages → meaning from flags, greetings, music & the L1 prompt. Non-readers/non-writers. Attention 5–7 min. Group = 12.

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song, “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the bus-box & shakes hands with each child.
  • Remember time — cumulative review (thumb-up/down Q&A like a game) with A4 flashcards / memory / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, one active game.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheets, a different type each lesson (sort & stick / colour / match / listen & tick / craft / draw & tell / passport), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
