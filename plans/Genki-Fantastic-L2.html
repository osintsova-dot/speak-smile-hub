Fantastic level · Lesson 2 of 6✨ languages + Can you speak…? Yes I can / No I can't12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to ask & answer about languages — Can you speak English? — Yes, I can! / No, I can't. — naming languages (English · Russian · French · Chinese · Japanese) and greeting in each.
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time cumulative recycle of earlier levels (incl. Fruit Market L1); folder worksheet (listen & tick — послушай и отметь); a multi-hello mingle.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; celebrate every home language in the room; every child says can / can't every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • ask Can you speak French?;
  • answer Yes, I can! / No, I can't. with a thumb up/down;
  • name 4–5 languages by ear;
  • say hello in several languages (Bonjour · Nǐ hǎo · Konnichiwa · Привет);
  • recycle What's this? — It's an apple!

TPR gesture
speak = hand flapping open/close at mouth · Yes I can = thumbs up + nod · No I can't = thumbs down + head shake · wave for each hello.
Pron. watch
can't (short, firm) vs can; keep Yes, I can! bright & proud. The hellos are for fun — accept any attempt.
Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
CCQ: (thumb up) “Can or can't?” → can. (thumb down) → can't.
ICQ: “When I ask, do you say YES with a thumb up or DOWN?” → thumb up.

Assumptions: children had Adventure→Fantastic L1; know Can you…? from superhero/actions. Languages are new but fun; thumbs anchor can/can't. Many children speak Russian at home — celebrate it. Near-zero English + L1 prompt. Non-readers. Group = 12.

Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Cumulative Q&A recycle lives at the start (Remember time); the lesson ends on a topic game, then the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives from the bus-box & shakes hands with each child (Hello! How are you?).
  • Remember time ⚡ — cumulative review of EVERYTHING so far: Q&A chain + a quick recall game (What's left? / board app).
  • Focus time ⚡ — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, 2–3 active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheet, a different type each lesson, always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Final game ⚡ — back to the carpet for a fast, fun game on today's language, ending on a high note.
  • Closing — Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
