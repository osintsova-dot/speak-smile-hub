🦝 Give Me Five! 1 · Unit 5 “Fun on the farm” · Lesson 1 — Vocabulary
Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., emergent readers · continuing line) · 60 min · PPP + TPR + Phonics · carpet + desks
Week 11.01–17.01✨ NEW · 10 farm animals~12 pupils · 3 teams of 4Pupil's Book p.50 · AB p.48🦸 Phonics Hero ур.25b · /и:/ ee·ea·e-e·y
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 10 farm animals and ask & answer using “Can you see a (cow)? — Yes, I can!” in the farm context.
  • Subsidiary. Sing the coursebook song On the farm; retrieve colours, numbers 1–10 & “Is it a…? — Yes, it is / No, it isn't” (U2) woven onto the animals; phonics — Phonics Hero ур.25 (b) закрепление: sound /и:/ in its houses ee · ea · e-e · y (word grid → sort → dictation → story «Счастливый щенок»); tricky words love, another; pre-literacy & fine motor (sticker chant).
  • Personal. Keep teacher-talk low; ICQ before every task; run a stirrer→settler rhythm with songs & signals; catch & recast the sheep/ship long /iː/.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name the 10 farm animals (cat · cow · sheep · goat · horse · duck · frog · bird · mouse · chicken);
  • ask & answer “Can you see a…? — Yes, I can!”;
  • read & sort /и:/ words by their houses (ee·ea·y), write bee, sea, meat, happy from dictation.

Structure
Can you see a (cow)? — Yes, I can!  ·  What's this? — It's a (…)  ·  recycle: The (duck) is (yellow). Is it a (goat)? — Yes, it is.
Form
Can + you + see + a + noun? → Yes, I + can. Weak “Can you” /kən jə/; strong can in the answer.
Pron. watch
long /iː/ sheep (not “ship”); /aʊ/ in cow; /tʃ/ in chicken; “one sheep, two sheep” (no plural -s).
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.25 (b) закрепление: sound /и:/ — houses ee (bee, tree) · ea (sea, meat) · e-e (these) · y at the end (happy) — word grid, sort, dictation bee, sea, meat, happy, слова-молнии these, theme, three, story «Счастливый щенок» (тетрадь стр.70–71). Tricky words: love · another.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Is the cow there, in the picture? Can I find it?” → yes → “Yes, I can!”
“bee, sea, happy — same sound /iː/? → yes! Same letters? → NO — different houses!”

Assumptions: colours, numbers 1–10, “Is it a…? — Yes, it is / No, it isn't” (U2) and all 26 phonic sounds + digraphs + Magic E are known (continuing line, mid-year); the /и:/ houses (ур.25a, стр.69) were met on the pre-holiday review lesson → after the break, start with a quick house recall. Pupils are non-/emergent readers → rely on pictures & audio, not printed text. Carpet & team routines are known.

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
sheep heard/said as “ship”Exaggerate long /iː/ with a smile-mouth gesture; drill sheep–seat–meat together.
Plural “sheeps / two sheep-s”Hold up 2 fingers: one sheep, two sheep — same word.
Confuse frog ↔ dog (Toby the pet is on the page)Clarify the ten; one clear gesture for frog (jump), name Toby separately as “a dog, our friend”.
‘ea’ read as two sounds /e/+/a/Slide two fingers together: two letters, ONE sound — /iː/.
Over-excited animal noises don't stopFreeze! + volume-control arm; follow every stirrer with a settler.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → pupils stop & echo “Yes!”
“WiFi” 🖐 — say “WiFi” + raise a spread hand → pupils silently mirror & tune in.
Volume control — raise/lower your arm = louder/quieter → settler.
“Give me five” — eyes · ears · mouth · hands · feet.
Countdown — “Finishing in 5…4…3…2…1… pencils down.”
“Freeze!” — statue, mid-movement reset.

10–22′
Phonics Hero — /и:/ ee·ea·e-e·y (b)
board → desks

Recall the houses (after the holidays — стр.69). One sound /iː/ — FOUR houses! 🏠 ee — bee, tree · 🏠 ea — sea, meat · 🏠 e-e — these · 🏠 hvostik y — happy! Quick chorus of each house's words. ▶ Take Off–Touch Down: Stand up if you hear /iː/… sea!… cat!… happy! — everyone reacts every time.

Word grid — тетрадь стр.70 №1 «Читаем слова». Read together, picture beside each: bee · tree · green · sea · meat · leaf · happy · funny. Kagan — RallyRobin: pairs take turns down the grid.

Sort — стр.70 №2 «Раздели слова» (all vote first). Nine words (tree · sea · happy · green · meat · funny · bee · leaf · sunny) → for each, every child points to the right house card (ee / ea / y) → check → write it in the column. Do 6 in class, 3 go home.

Dictation — стр.70 №3 (everyone writes). Now YOU write. Listen: bee… word once → segment on fingers → all write → Show me! → reveal & self-check. Words: bee · sea · meat · happy. Then слова-молнии (стр.70 №4): these · theme · three — speed strip, there and back; + tricky words love · another — These are tricky — we just know them!

Story — стр.71 «Счастливый щенок» (settler). Read the 5 frames like a comic, chorus each line: A happy puppy. The puppy is funny… ⭐ Star task: find & circle every puppy (4). Colour the star «Я молодец!» (стр.70).

💪 Support: pre-write the house letters on their line; they copy the word under. ⭐ Extension: add one more /iː/ word (read · eat).

✔ Assess: the “Show me!” pages are your live check — scan for who mixes the ee/ea houses; recast on the spot.

24–33′
Presentation · Vocabulary
carpet → teams

Present — 10 farm animals. Hold up each A4 card: What's this? If unknown, model: It's a cow. Cow. Choral drill ×3, then 2–3 children solo. Cards: cat · cow · sheep · goat · horse · duck · frog · bird · mouse · chicken.

🎮 Interactive (projector): “What's this?” spinner — колесо животных. Spin → a farm animal lands. A rotating child taps to spin; whole class answers chorally It's a (sheep)! Predict: before the reveal — Is it a horse? — Yes/No! (recycles U2). Every child shows a thumb 👍/👎 = all engaged.

▶ Fan-N-Pick (teams of 4, mini-cards): ① fans the cards · ② picks & asks What's this? · ③ answers It's a (duck). · ④ praises Well done! Rotate roles every turn — everyone asks, answers, praises.

ICQ: Do you say “What's this?” or “How many?” (→ What's this?)

💪 Support: pair a shy child with the “praiser” role first. ⭐ Extension: answer in a chunk — It's a small grey mouse. 		A4 flashcards ×10 · 3 mini-sets · What's this? spinner (board) · ▶ Fan-N-Pick

44–52′
Ask & Find
carpet, in pairs
🎮 Interactive (projector): “Find it!” — farm scene. Tap Speaky — 🦝 asks Can you see a (goat)? the class scans the farm on screen, points & answers Yes, I can! (2–3 rounds, to model the phrase for all).

▶ Memory · Can you see a…? — in pairs, on the carpet. Each pair has 5 animal cards. Child 1 shows a card, names it — It's a cow. — and places it face-down. Repeat for all 5 (show → name → place).

Then Child 1 asks: Can you see a cow? Child 2 — from memory, first try — turns the right card. Correct → Yes, I can! and keeps it. Then swap roles. (Most cards wins.) Reset if loud: Freeze!

✔ Assess: stand behind one pair at a time — can they both produce the question AND the answer unprompted? That's the lesson's success check.

💪 Support: 3 cards, not 5; keep the animal name on a small strip. ⭐ Extension: add a “no” card & No, I can't! (previews L2). 		5 flashcards per pair · Find it! (board, model) · Speaky
