Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 21.09–27.09 · Занятие 6✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.6 · u·l·f
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to ask and answer about age: “How old are you? — I'm (7) years old!” + “Happy Birthday!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.6: sounds u·l·f, vowel gap-fill, dictation; recycle numbers 1–12 (зан.5) inside the age context — candles, cakes, survey.
  • Personal. Run the class survey so EVERY child asks and answers many times — not one-by-one to the teacher.
By the end, pupils can…
  • answer “How old are you?” with “I'm (7) years old”;
  • ask the question to classmates in a mingle survey;
  • read and write sun, cup, fun, log; fill the missing vowel (u/e/o).
Target language

Structure
How old are you? — I'm (7) years old. · Happy Birthday! · How many candles on your cake?
Form
I'm + number + years old (chunk!). “years old” can drop early on — build it back in.
Pron. watch
old /əʊld/ with the dark l; linking seven-years-old; question melody rises on OLD.
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.6 (тетрадь стр.15–16): u /а — зонт вверх!/ · l /л — язычок вверх/ · f /ф — рыбка/ · blending sun, cup, leg, log, mug, fun · впиши гласную (u/e/o) · диктант sun, cup, fun, log · tricky have, we.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“How old are you? — do I ask about your NAME or your AGE?” → age (show candles).
“I'm seven years old — сколько свечек на торте?” → seven!

Assumptions: From занятие 5: numbers 1–12 + How many? (fresh — recycle hard); cake drawings saved from зан.5. Sounds s→o known; u·l·f new. Children know the survey mingle format from Mix-Pair-Share.

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Answer collapses to just «seven»Accept, then rebuild the chunk: “Seven! I'm seven years old!” — child echoes.
u — sounds like Russian «у»Model /ʌ/ with the umbrella-up gesture: “a-a-up!”; contrast cup–кубок kindly.
Same-age group → survey feels sameyAdd Speaky (he's 5!), teacher, puppets with silly ages (12!) to vary answers.
Vowel gap-fill u/e/o confusionSay the word slowly, hand on chin — mouth shape tells the vowel; partner check.
Birthday excitement (whose birthday is real!)Channel it: the child with the nearest birthday blows the pretend candles first.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → pupils stop & echo “Yes!”
“WiFi” 🖐 — say “WiFi” + raise a spread hand → pupils silently mirror & tune in.
Volume control — raise/lower your arm = louder/quieter → settler.
“Give me five” — eyes · ears · mouth · hands · feet.
Countdown — “Finishing in 5…4…3…2…1… pencils down.”
“Freeze!” — statue, mid-movement reset.

Phonics Hero ур.6 — u·l·f
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.15 №1. Strip s→o (15 sounds). There and back, beat your time.

Meet u · l · f. u — зонт вверх-вниз: a-a! (up!) · l — язычок вверх, поём: l-l-l! (облизываем леденец) · f — рыбка губами: fff! Copy ×3, mixed flash.

Blend — стр.16 №1. Windows: /s/ /u/ /n/ — sun! /c/ /u/ /p/ — cup! … leg, log, mug, fun.

Trace — стр.15 (desks). Прописи u · l · f · sun · leg; whisper the sounds.

Gap-fill — стр.16 №2 (all vote). Picture + s_n: say the word slowly, hand on chin — which vowel? Every child air-writes the letter, then fills: sun, cup, leg, mug. Partner check (RallyCoach).

Dictation — стр.16 №3. Segment → write → Show me! Words: sun, cup, fun, log. Tricky strip have · we (and notice — we from зан.4!). Medal ⭐.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «g o u l f b»: «Звуки» + «Блендинг». ◆ Support: gap-fill with the vowel bank visible. ▲ Extension: extra word mug by ear. ✔ Assess: chin-hand vowel check + Show-me boards.
