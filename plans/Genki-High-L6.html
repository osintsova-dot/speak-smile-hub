🦝 GenkiEnglish Preschool · High · Lesson 6 — What's your favourite colour?
Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
High level · Lesson 6 of 9 · final year✨ My favourite colour is… · +gold/silver12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to ask & answer What's your favourite colour? — My favourite colour is purple! across the rainbow (+ gold, silver).
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time recycle of colours/pirates & transport; the S&S big-book; a draw-&-tell folder sheet (my favourite colour).
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; colours always in context via the favourite question; every child chooses/says every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name rainbow colours + gold/silver
  • answer What's your favourite colour? — My favourite colour is blue!
  • run to their favourite-colour corner & say it
  • ask a friend in a mingle survey
  • draw & tell their favourite colour.

Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song, "choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair".
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the bus-box & shakes hands with each child.
  • Remember time — cumulative review (thumb-up/down Q&A like a game) with A4 flashcards / memory / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, one active game.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheets, a different type each lesson (draw & tell / match & stick / order / colour by command / listen & tick / craft), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🟢 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
