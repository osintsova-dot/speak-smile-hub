Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will have consolidated Unit 7 — rooms, There's/are (±), possessive pronouns — and shown it in the flagship Team Quiz with reward game.
  • Subsidiary. PB Review p.80 + House dice game (AB p.77 format); spelling; traffic light.
  • Personal. Error harvest (There's for plurals, mine+noun, his/hers) into tiles; shooter rotation; pupil scorekeeper.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name/spell unit words;
  • run There-grammar + possessives under game pressure;
  • self-assess honestly.
