Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to follow, read and act out the story «The four seasons» and ask about habits: “Do they (celebrate Halloween)? — Yes, they do. / No, they don't.”
  • Subsidiary. Story cycle; seasons project inside the story (kids photograph the year); cultural seed — chestnuts in America?; they-questions complete the do-machine.
  • Personal. The story is a year in fast-forward — stage it as a journey through four “season stations” in the room.
By the end, pupils can…
  • retell the story season by season;
  • ask & answer “Do they…? — Yes, they do / No, they don't”;
  • read their role's lines.

Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Do they celebrate Halloween? — answer with do or is?” → do: Yes, they do!
“The story goes through how many seasons?” → four — a whole year!

Assumptions: Do-questions warm from L2; seasons vivid. Story format loved and routine; “season stations” staging is new — explain once, it runs itself. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).
