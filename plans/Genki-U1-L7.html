🦝 GenkiEnglish Preschool · Unit 1 “Start speaking” · Lesson 7 — Seasons & Revision of Unit 1
Pre-A1 preschoolers (~5 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + games + TPR (no reading / writing) · chairs + carpet + tables
Week 21.09–27.09 · Lesson 7 of 72✨ seasons + pronoun review12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 All by ear — no text, no phonics
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to name the 4 seasons & say a season chunk — It's summer! · It's winter! · What season is it? · I love autumn! — recognising summer · autumn · winter · spring by picture & mime.
  • Subsidiary. Weave in L5 pronouns (We love summer! Everybody loves winter!) so review lives inside the new topic; a language-linked craft settler (season sort / spinning season-wheel — say as you sort); a draw-&-tell finish — draw your favourite season & tell I love…!
  • Personal. Every child moves/speaks every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘; hold to max 3 game-peaks; keep seasons concrete via weather-mime, not abstract calendar talk.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name the 4 seasons from a picture;
  • say It's summer! / It's winter!;
  • answer What season is it?;
  • say I love…! / We love summer! (pronoun review);
  • draw & tell a favourite season to teacher/partner.

Chunks
It's summer! · It's winter! · What season is it? · I love autumn!  ·  (recycle) We love summer! · Everybody loves winter!
TPR gesture
summer = fan yourself / swim · autumn = falling leaves fingers · winter = shiver / catch snow · spring = flowers growing up. Each season = one clear weather-mime.
Pron. watch
It's as one word /ɪts/; autumn silent n /ˈɔːtəm/; summer short /ʌ/; keep I love… a warm, felt phrase (hand on heart).
Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ (four corners): “When the music stops — run to the season Speaky calls?” → yes!
ICQ (mime): “Do the weather AND… say the season?” → yes!

Assumptions: children have had L1–L6; pronouns from L5 are fresh → perfect to recycle here. Seasons are abstract — anchor to weather-mime & pictures, not to months/calendar. Near-zero English beyond unit chunks · non-readers/non-writers · attention 5–7 min · group = 12, whole-group + shoulder-partner. Four-corners uses the whole room (everyone moves).

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Seasons feel abstractTeach each as a WEATHER-MIME (summer = swim, winter = shiver) + a big picture card. The body makes it real, not the calendar.
Autumn/spring hard to sayAccept the mime + a close attempt; the song fixes pronunciation over time. Praise the gesture.
Four-corners → running/collisionsBubble-arms, walk not run to the corner; Freeze! between rounds; corners clearly marked with big season pictures.
Pronoun review forgottenRe-model with L5 gestures (point self / friends / all) inside the season chant — no separate drill.
Over-excitement after ⚡🧘 settler after each ⚡; volume-control arm.
TransitionsTrain Come with me! 🚂 — one signal, one destination.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter.
“Speaky says…” — do it only when Speaky says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone freezes, reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…”.
“Come with me!” 🚂 — train to change zone.
Lesson framework — the shape of EVERY Genki preschool lesson (all 72)

Fixed skeleton — reuse this order in every plan. Keep the ⚡/🧘 rhythm, but max 2–3 game-peaks per lesson — don't over-game. Review is woven into the new topic (pronouns applied to seasons).

  • 1 · Greeting 🚪 🧘 — door welcome + Speaky arrives.
  • 2 · Warm-up song ⚡ — Genki Disco Warm Up (ritual + recycles earlier language).
  • 3 · Core games ⚡/🧘 — 2–3 games max, each with its song.
  • 4 · Craft / worksheet block 🧘 — language-linked. Here: season sort / wheel.
  • 5 · 📖 Story time 🧘 — S&S big-book (Speaky), choral refrain.
  • 6 · Workbook worksheet — draw & tell 🧘.
  • 7 · Reflection + Goodbye + Exit 🧘.

Well done! Come with me! 🚂 To the tables. Language link — listen → do → say. Give each child an S&S season-wheel base (4 quarters: sun · leaves · snow · flowers) + small picture cut-outs (swimsuit · leaf · snowman · flower).

Mechanic. Speaky calls a season — Find… summer! — each child sorts the matching picture onto the right quarter & the class choruses It's summer! Work through all four; add a spinner arrow. Sort → say — every child engaged.

Countdown: 5… 4… 3… 2… 1… stick it down.
