🦝 GenkiEnglish Preschool · Fantastic · Lesson 52 — I can do it! Can you play the piano?
Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
Fantastic level · Lesson 4 of 6✨ 5 instruments · Can you play the…?12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to name 5 instruments (piano, guitar, drums, violin, recorder) and ask & answer Can you play the piano? — Yes, I can do it! — miming each instrument.
  • Subsidiary. Recycle the football Can you…? (Lesson 51) with a new answer I can do it!; the S&S big-book; a make-a-mini-instrument craft at Table time.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; instruments always with the mime (play & say together, not a bare list); everyone plays every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name 5 instruments;
  • ask Can you play the guitar?;
  • answer Yes, I can do it!;
  • mime playing each one;
  • make & play a mini instrument.

Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: “Do we MIME playing AND say it?” → yes.
ICQ: “Yes = I can do it!, No = …?” → No, I can’t.

Assumptions: children can ask Can you…? from Lessons 50–51 but are near-zero English for instruments → meaning from mimes, real/toy instruments, music & the L1 prompt. Non-readers/non-writers. Attention 5–7 min. Group = 12.

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song, “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the bus-box & shakes hands with each child.
  • Remember time — cumulative review (thumb-up/down Q&A like a game) with A4 flashcards / memory / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, one active game.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheets, a different type each lesson (sort & stick / colour / match / draw & tell / craft / listen & tick / passport), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
