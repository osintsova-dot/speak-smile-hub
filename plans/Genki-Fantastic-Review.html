Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will consolidate the whole Fantastic level — What's this? (fruit) · Can you speak…? · Can you kick? · Can you play the piano? · Where are you from? — recalling and using all five with confidence.
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time cumulative recycle of all earlier levels too; review worksheet (listen & circle — послушай и обведи, mixed topics); the flagship team quiz.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; low-pressure, celebratory; every child answers every round; teams are for fun, everyone “wins”; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • answer all 5 Fantastic questions in a mixed chain;
  • sing the medley of Fantastic songs with gestures;
  • match fruit / flags / instruments by ear;
  • play in a team quiz using the target chunks;
  • show off their year on the folder award grid.

Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ (team quiz): “Do we shout out, or wait for our team's turn?” → wait for the turn.
ICQ: “When my team scores, does the OTHER team clap too?” → yes, we all cheer!

Assumptions: children completed Fantastic L1–L5. Review = recall + celebrate, never test-pressure. The team quiz reward (World Cup Soccer!) is the only scored quiz in the level — kept light, both teams cheered. Near-zero English + L1 prompt. Non-readers. Group = 12.

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages (review edition)

Practice-proven skeleton — review edition. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Remember time recycles all levels; Focus becomes a Fantastic medley + games; the big team quiz with reward lives here (Review only).

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives from the bus-box & shakes hands with each child (Hello! How are you?).
  • Remember time ⚡ — cumulative review of EVERYTHING (all levels) via a mixed Q&A chain + recall game.
  • Focus / medley ⚡ — sing through all 5 Fantastic songs; quick game per topic (fruit · languages · football · instruments · flags).
  • Table time 🪑 — review worksheet: listen & circle (mixed topics), listen → do → say.
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky): a “round-the-world adventure” touching every Fantastic topic.
  • Team quiz + Final 🏆 — the flagship World Cup Soccer! team quiz with animated reward, ending on a high note.
  • Closing — achievement check, Thank You! + Goodbye song, big reward sticker on the folder.
