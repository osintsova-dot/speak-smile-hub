Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will have consolidated the whole Fantastic level — fruit market (What would you like?), languages & countries (Can you speak…? / Where are you from?), football & instruments (Can you kick? / play the piano?) — in a joyful flagship quiz + station rotation.
  • Subsidiary. Confidence & fluency across all 5 topics; peer support in teams; celebrate progress with a reward.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; everyone answers every question (whole-team choral, not one child) so no queue; keep the quiz light & kind — every team wins a cheer; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • run the fruit market;
  • ask & answer Can you…?;
  • say Where are you from? — I’m from…;
  • name fruit, languages, actions, instruments, countries;
  • play the quiz & win the reward.

Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: “Does the WHOLE team answer together?” → yes (no single child on the spot).
ICQ: “Right answer → one roll / one shot?” → yes.

Assumptions: children have met all 5 Fantastic topics (Lessons 49–53). This is consolidation, not new input — keep it playful & kind; the flagship quiz + reward belongs to Review only. Non-readers/non-writers. Attention 5–7 min. Group = 12.

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Losing team upsetBoth teams roll/shoot every round; keep scores close; end with a joint cheer — everyone wins a sticker.
One child answers, others waitWhole team huddles & shouts together; Numbered Heads — any child could be picked, so all prepare.
Too much at onceGroup cards by topic; one topic at a time on the quiz; the songs cue each set.
Over-excitement🧘 settler between rounds; Freeze! + volume-control arm + countdown.
Station chaosSmall groups rotate on a bell; Speaky & teacher each hold a station.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Review shape. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Review keeps the ritual bookends but the core is the flagship quiz + station rotation. Reward belongs to Review only.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song, choose the greeting → chair.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up; Speaky arrives on the bus-box & shakes hands.
  • Remember time — lightning recap of all 5 topics with the songs medley & board app.
  • Flagship quiz 🏆 — two teams, Numbered Heads, right answer → a bowling roll / football shot; the level’s ONE competitive game.
  • Station rotation 🪑 — small groups rotate through 3 topic stations (market / band / passport).
  • Reading time 🌱 — class chooses a favourite S&S big-book from the level.
  • Closing — big cumulative chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, reward + certificate on the folder.
