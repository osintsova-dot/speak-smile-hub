🦝 Give Me Five! 1 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 3 / Intro L3 — Superhero: I can…
Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 07.09–13.09 · Занятие 3✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.3 · c·k·e
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 8 action verbs and say what they can do: “I'm a superhero! I can (fly)!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.3: sounds c·k·e + the “ck” secret; blend cat, pen, ten…; recycle feelings + greetings from занятие 2 (a superhero is happy / tired after flying!).
  • Personal. TPR-heavy lesson — plan the space; use Freeze! early and often, follow every stirrer with a settler.
By the end, pupils can…
  • mime and name jump · run · hide · cook · stretch · climb · swim · fly;
  • say “I can (swim)!” about themselves;
  • read and write cat, pen, ten; explain the c·k·ck secret.
Target language

Structure
I'm a superhero! · I can (jump / fly / swim)!
Form
I + can + verb (no “to”, no ending!). can = умею/могу.
Pron. watch
silent b in climb /klaɪm/; stretch final /tʃ/; sentence stress: I can FLY!
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.3 (тетрадь стр.8–9): c · k · e — звук /к/ пишется c и k, а вместе ck! · blending cat, pen, ten, neck, pet, tick · «обведи слово» · диктант cat, pen, ten, kit · tricky said, he.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“I can swim — do I do it now, or am I ABLE to?” → able to (умею).
“c and k — two letters… how many sounds?” → ONE: /к/!

Assumptions: From занятия 1–2: commands, Freeze!, names, greetings, feelings, sounds s·a·t·i·p·n + blending CVC. Non-readers apart from phonics work; all meaning through mime and pictures.

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
climb said with /b/Exaggerate /klaɪm/ with a climbing mime; quick choral drill of the pair climb–time.
can + «to» or Russian mix («I can прыгать»)Recast instantly with the mime: “I can JUMP!” — child echoes.
TPR chaos: children crash into each otherFix personal “superhero spots”; actions on the spot; Freeze! resets.
c/k/ck — when to write whichToday just the secret + chorus; the sort will recur in ур.10–12 — don't over-explain.
Shy children won't mime aloneAll mimes are whole-class or pairs; nobody performs solo unless they volunteer.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → pupils stop & echo “Yes!”
“WiFi” 🖐 — say “WiFi” + raise a spread hand → pupils silently mirror & tune in.
Volume control — raise/lower your arm = louder/quieter → settler.
“Give me five” — eyes · ears · mouth · hands · feet.
Countdown — “Finishing in 5…4…3…2…1… pencils down.”
“Freeze!” — statue, mid-movement reset.

Phonics Hero ур.3 — c·k·e
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.8 №1. Strip s·a·t·i·p·n: there and back, beat your time; whisper → LOUD.

Meet c · k · e. c — кастаньеты-крабик пальцами: k-k-k! · k — same sound, каратэ-рука: k-k! · e — разбиваем яйцо о край: e-e-e! The secret (Spiky line): звук /к/ пишется c и k — а вместе ck (neck, tick)! Copy sounds + gestures ×3.

Blend — стр.9 №1. Sound windows: /c/ /a/ /t/ — cat! /p/ /e/ /n/ — pen! … ten, neck, pet, tick — air-blend fingers, picture for meaning.

Trace — стр.8 (desks). Прописи c · k · e · cat · pen, start at the yellow dot, whisper the sounds.

Circle the word — стр.9 №2 (all vote). Picture up (cat) + three words (cat/cap/can) → read all three together → every child points/circles the right one; partner check. Same for pen, ten, neck.

Dictation — стр.9 №3. Segment on fingers → write → Show me! Words: cat, pen, ten, kit. Tricky strip said · he — flash, chorus ×3, air-write. Medal ⭐.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «c k e h r m d»: Stations «Звуки» + «Блендинг» on the projector; offline = тетрадь + cards. ◆ Support: dictation with first letter given. ▲ Extension: write neck — remember ck! ✔ Assess: Show-me boards; who blends /к/ twice in neck — recast.
