🎅 GenkiEnglish Preschool · Christmas · Lesson 1 (L27) — Genki Christmas — snowman, Santa, reindeer, tree, stocking, candy cane, presents
Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
Christmas block · Lesson 1 of 7✨ 7 Christmas words · the big Christmas song12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to recognise & name 7 Christmas words (snowman, Santa Claus, reindeer, Christmas tree, Christmas stocking, candy cane, presents) and sing the big Genki Christmas song with gestures.
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time recycle of greetings & numbers (How many presents? — Six!); the S&S big-book; a Christmas-tree ornament craft (make & name).
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; new words always in context (Look! A snowman!, not a bare list); every child moves/points/says every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘; keep the festive magic warm.
By the end, pupils can…
  • point to & name 7 Christmas things;
  • sing the Genki Christmas song with actions;
  • answer What’s this? — A snowman!;
  • run & touch the right Christmas card;
  • name one Christmas word at the door.

ICQ: “Touch AND say the word?” → yes.

Assumptions: children know greetings, numbers & earlier topics but Christmas words are new → meaning from festive props, gestures, the song & the L1 prompt. It is the season’s first Christmas lesson — aim for joyful recognition, not mastery. Non-readers/non-writers (no phonics). Attention 5–7 min → ⚡/🧘 rhythm. Group = 12.

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Long words (“Christmas stocking”) hard to saySay word + gesture together 3×; let the song carry it; accept the gesture + first sound at first.
Over-excited by presents/Santa🧘 settler after each ⚡; Freeze! statue + volume-control arm; Speaky whispers.
“reindeer” → “raindeer/reneer”Antlers gesture + slow “RAIN-deer”; over-do it playfully.
A child just watchesFine — Speaky waves, offers a card to hold; most join by the song.
Transitions get chaoticTrain: Come with me! — one signal, one destination.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Festive touch: Speaky wears a Santa hat & the bus-box gets a New Year hat. Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the festive bus-box & shakes hands (Hello! Merry Christmas!).
  • Remember time — cumulative review (greetings & numbers) with A4 flashcards / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box (Santa’s sack), Christmas song, board app, active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder / craft, a different type each lesson; today a Christmas-tree ornament craft, always language-linked (make → hang → say).
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
