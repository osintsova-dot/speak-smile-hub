🦝 GenkiEnglish Preschool · High · Lesson 2 — When is your birthday? (Months Jan–Jun)
Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
High level · Lesson 2 of 8✨ months Jan–Jun + birthday12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to name months Jan–Jun and say when their birthday is — When is your birthday? — My birthday is in May.
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time cumulative recycle of the whole year + last lesson's sports; folder worksheet (match — соедини cake to month).
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; anchor months to the year-calendar wall & a birthday-cake picture; accept the month word alone before the full chunk; every child speaks every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • say months: January · February · March · April · May · June;
  • answer When is your birthday? — My birthday is in…;
  • find their own birthday month;
  • line up / group by birthday month.

TPR gesture
hold up a birthday-cake mime + point to the month on the big year-calendar; clap the rhythm of each month name.
Pron. watch
keep -uary / -ember light; clap the syllables (Feb-ru-a-ry). One clear month + my birthday is in… is the win.
Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: “Say the month AND clap it?” → yes.
ICQ: (Mingle) “Find friends with the SAME birthday month — talk or push?” → talk.

Assumptions: children have done the whole Genki year + L1 sports. Most know their own birthday in L1 → we bridge to the English month. Non-readers → year-calendar is pictures + Speaky points. Group = 12.

Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.

Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). The whole cumulative Q&A recycle lives at the start (Remember time); the finale is a fast game on today's language + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → move to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives & shakes hands with each child (Hello! How are you?).
  • Remember time — cumulative review of the whole year with A4 flashcards / grab game / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheet, a different type each lesson (listen & tick / colour / match / draw & tell / put-in-order / mini-craft), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🟢 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Final game ⚡ — back to the carpet for a fast, fun game on today's language, so the lesson ends on a high note.
  • Closing — Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
