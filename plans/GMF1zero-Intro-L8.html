🦝 Give Me Five! 1 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 8 / Intro L8 — Colours + What's your favourite colour?
Pre-A1 young learners (~7 y.o., first-year beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 28.09–04.10 · Занятие 8✨ NEW~12 pupils · pairs & teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.8 · w·x·y + история про лиса
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to name 10+ colours and answer “What's your favourite colour? — It's (red)! / I like (blue)!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.8: sounds w·x·y, yes/no reading task, story «Лис и фургон»; recycle Do you like…? (зан.7) and numbers (six blue spots!) with the new colour words.
  • Personal. Colours live on REAL things — clothes, crayons, the room; never drill colour cards alone.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name red · orange · yellow · green · blue · pink · purple · black · white · brown (+ gold, silver, bronze for fun);
  • answer “What's your favourite colour?” and ask a friend;
  • read and write web, box, six, yes; answer yes/no to short written sentences.

Structure

Form
favourite = самый любимый (ONE!). It's + colour as the short answer chunk.
Pron. watch
purple /ˈpɜːpl/ two beats; yellow /ˈjeləʊ/ (not «еллоу» with hard л); favourite — 2 beats: FAV-rit.
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.8 (тетрадь стр.20–22): w /у-волны/ · x /кс/ · y /й/ · blending web, fox, box, six, wig, yes · «да/нет» по прочитанному · диктант web, box, six, yes · tricky go, my · история «Лис и фургон» (стр.22).
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“Favourite — many colours or ONE best?” → one best (hug gesture).
“x — one letter… how many sounds?” → TWO: /к//с/ — fo-x!

Assumptions: From занятия 1–7: Do you like…? + Yes/No chunks (fresh from зан.7 — recycle hard), numbers, feelings. Sounds s→v; CVC routine solid; several colour words may be half-known from cartoons. Группы: команда — максимум 4; при другой численности делим на команды по 3–4 (12=3×4 · 10=3+3+4 · 9=3×3 · 8=2×4 · 7=3+4).

Phonics Hero ур.8 — w·x·y
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.20 №1. Strip s→v (21 sound!). Beat your time; silly voices.

Meet w · x · y. w — волны руками: w-w! · x — рентген-каратэ: кс-кс! (одна буква — ДВА звука!) · y — йо-йо: й-й! Copy ×3, mixed flash.

Blend — стр.21 №1. Windows: /w/ /e/ /b/ — web! /f/ /o/ /x/ — fox! … box, six, wig, yes.

Trace — стр.20 (desks). Прописи w · x · y · web · fox; whisper.

Yes/no — стр.21 №2 (all vote). Picture + sentence It is a fox. — choral read, every child votes thumbs up/down, then circles yes/no. 4 sentences; partner check.

Dictation — стр.21 №3. Segment → write → Show me! Words: web, box, six, yes. Tricky strip go · my (my — из «MY name is»!). Medal ⭐.

Story — стр.22 «Лис и фургон» (settler). Echo-read 5 frames: A red van. A fox jumps in the van… ⭐ count the foxes — and say the VAN's colour: It's RED! — сегодняшняя тема уже в истории.
