🦝 Give Me Five! 2 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 2 / Intro L2 — What's your name? + Rock Paper Scissors
Pre-A1 young learners (~8–9 y.o., zero beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 07.09–13.09 · Занятие 2✨ NEW · introductions~10 pupils · pairs & 2 teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.2 · i·p·n
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to introduce themselves and ask a partner's name: “What's your name? — My name is… Nice to meet you!” — and play Rock Paper Scissors in English.
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.2: sounds i·p·n → first dictation + FIRST STORY read; keep the Disco ritual alive.
  • Personal. Back-chain the question; keep every child talking every round (mingle, not queue); losers cheer winners — set the game culture.
By the end, pupils can…
  • ask & answer What's your name? — My name is […]!;
  • play Rock Paper Scissors with the chant, win/lose politely;
  • blend and write pin, tap, ant, sit — first dictation + first story!

Structure

Form
What + 's (is) + your name? Answer builds from name-only to the full “My name is…”.
Pron. watch
“name” /eɪ/ not /а/; rhythm WHAT's your NAME? (clap the two beats); scissors /ˈsɪzəz/.
Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.2 (тетрадь стр.5–7): звуки i·p·n + жесты · блендинг 6 звуков (pin, tap, pan, nap, tin, sit) · ПЕРВЫЙ диктант · история «Моя первая история!» · tricky I, is.
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“When I ask What's your name? — do you say Hello or your name?” → your name.
“You lose at Rock Paper Scissors — what do you say?” → Good game! (and cheer).

Assumptions: Занятие 1 done: 7 commands, Freeze!, carpet spots, s·a·t. Names were said informally — today the full question is taught properly.

Phonics Hero ур.2 — i·p·n
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.5 №1. Strip s · a · t: There and back — fast! Boys… girls… whisper… LOUD!

Meet i · p · n. i — мышка: i-i-i! (пальчики-усики) · p — задуваем свечку: p-p-p! · n — самолёт гудит: nnn! Copy sound + gesture ×3; mixed flash.

Blend — стр.6 №1 (шесть звуков — читаем целые слова!). Sound windows: /p/ /i/ /n/ — pin! /t/ /a/ /p/ — tap! … pan, nap, tin, sit. Every child air-blends with a finger.

Trace — стр.5 (desks). Прописи i · p · n · pin · nap — whisper as you write. Then «соедини» task: word → picture (ant, pan, tin, nap), partner check (RallyCoach).

FIRST DICTATION — стр.6 (everyone writes). Listen: /p/ /i/ /n/… pin! — segment on fingers → write → Show me! → reveal. Words: pin, tap, ant, sit. Tricky strip I · is — flash, chorus, air-write.

Story — стр.7 «Моя первая история!» (settler). Chorus the 5 frames, finger under the words: A pan. An ant is in the pan. The ant naps… — их первая прочитанная история! Big cheer 🎉 + medal ⭐.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «s a t i p n»: Station «Блендинг» (sound windows) + история on screen. ◆ Support: dictation with first letter given. ▲ Extension: 5th word by ear (tin). ✔ Assess: the Show-me boards on dictation = live check of every child.
