🦝 GenkiEnglish Preschool · High · Lesson 1 — What sports do you play?
Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
High level · Lesson 1 of 8✨ sports + I play…12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to name sports and say which they play — What sports do you play? — I play tennis / basketball / soccer. — using the mime for each sport as they say the word.
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time cumulative recycle of the WHOLE year (names, feelings, numbers, weather, likes, time, places…) as this is the last Genki level; folder worksheet (listen & tick).
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; big clear mimes (do WITH the class); accept single words Tennis! before the full chunk; every child moves/speaks every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name sports: tennis · soccer · basketball · volleyball · table tennis · badminton · rugby · baseball;
  • answer What sports do you play? — I play…;
  • ask a friend Do you play soccer?;
  • mime the sport while saying its name.

Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: “Do the sport AND say the word?” → yes.
ICQ: (Karuta) “Grab the card when you hear YOUR sport — push or grab gently?” → grab gently.

Assumptions: children have done the whole Genki year (Adventure→Giant) — lots of Q&A already known. Some know a sport or two from real life. Near-zero reading + L1 prompt. Non-readers → all by ear + mime. Group = 12.

Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). The whole cumulative Q&A recycle lives at the start (Remember time); the finale is a fast game on today's language + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives & shakes hands with each child (Hello! How are you?).
  • Remember time — cumulative review of the WHOLE year with A4 flashcards / grab game / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheet, a different type each lesson (listen & tick / colour by command / match / draw & tell / put-in-order / mini-craft), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🟢 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Final game ⚡ — back to the carpet for a fast, fun game on today's language, so the lesson ends on a high note.
  • Closing — Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
