Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will have consolidated the whole Experts level — directions, counting 1–100, money, shapes — used together in a joyful review with the flagship reward quiz.
  • Subsidiary. Team play & taking turns; the S&S big-book (favourite pick); a mixed-review station rotation at the tables.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; every question is answered CHORALLY by all 12 first, then a knock-down for a team point — no one just watches; the flagship quiz (bowling / basketball) lives ONLY in this review; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • give directions left / right / forward;
  • count 1–100 & countdown;
  • ask How much is it? & say cheap / expensive;
  • name 5 shapes;
  • use it all in the quiz & stations.

Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: “Do we ALL answer first, then bowl?” → yes, everyone answers.
ICQ: “Win or lose — do we cheer?” → yes, good job!

Assumptions: children have met all Experts topics (L42–47) → review is recall & celebration, not new input. Meaning from cards, objects, music & the L1 prompt. Non-readers/non-writers. Attention 6–8 min. Group = 12 (2 teams of 6).

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Losing team upsetEvery child answers & bowls in rotation; cheer BOTH teams; the prize is a group sticker, not one winner.
Waiting in line at the quizWhole group answers CHORALLY first; only the knock-down rotates — fast turns, keep it moving.
Over-excitement🧘 settler between rounds; Freeze! + volume-control arm.
Some topics forgottenRe-model quickly with the card/gesture, whole group echoes, then ask again — success for all.
Station chaosSmall groups, one adult signal, rotate by the train on the countdown.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Review-lesson skeleton. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). The flagship reward quiz (bowling / basketball) is used ONLY in this review. Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello / Good Morning song, “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the bus-box & shakes hands with each child.
  • Remember time — whole-level recall with A4 flashcards / app on the board.
  • Flagship quiz — Speaky’s Bowling / Basketball quiz across all Experts topics, in 2 teams.
  • Table time — stations 🪑 — mixed-review rotation (directions maze / count & colour / coins / shape collage).
  • Reading time 🌱 — favourite S&S big-book, read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + level-award sticker on the folder.
