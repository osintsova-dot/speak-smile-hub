Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will have consolidated the whole Experts level — Go left/right! · How many? · Count to twenty! · How much is it? · Blast off! · What shape is it? — used across a song medley, a team quiz & games, celebrating success.
  • Subsidiary. Achievement check (who can do what) via the flagship quiz; folder review worksheet (mixed listen & tick); a favourite big-book re-read.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; keep it a celebration, not a test; every child answers & moves every round; balanced teams; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • follow & give directions to the treasure;
  • count & answer How many? (1–20);
  • ask a price: How much is it?;
  • count in tens & blast off;
  • name shapes: What shape is it?

Anticipated problems & solutions
Likely problemWhat you do
Losing the quiz → tearsBoth teams cheer every goal; keep scores close; everyone gets a certificate & sticker.
Fast kids answer for everyoneWhole-team chorus answer; Numbered Heads — Speaky picks the number to say it.
Too much sitting in a quizEach answer has an action (jump/draw the shape/blast off); teams stand to answer.
Over-excitement after ⚡🧘 settler after each ⚡; volume-control arm.
Transitions get chaoticTrain: Come with me! — one signal, one destination.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “5… 4… 3… 2… 1…” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Review shape. Same skeleton, but Focus becomes a song medley of the whole level and the flagship team quiz with animated rewards (World Cup Soccer!) — used only on Review. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Ends on a high-note celebration.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives from the bus-box & shakes hands with each child (Hello! How are you?).
  • Remember time — cumulative Q&A across ALL topics + Genki Karuta on the board.
  • Focus · medley — a song medley of all six Experts songs with actions.
  • Flagship quiz — World Cup Soccer! team quiz with animated goal rewards (Review only).
  • Table time 🪑 — folder review worksheet (mixed listen & tick across topics).
  • Reading time 🌱 — a favourite S&S big-book, chosen by the class.
  • Final celebration game ⚡ + Closing — achievement check, certificates & stickers, Thank You! + Goodbye song.
