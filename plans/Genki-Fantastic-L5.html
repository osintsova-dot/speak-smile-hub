Genki preschoolers (~5–6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
Fantastic level · Lesson 5 of 6✨ countries + Where are you from? I'm from…12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to ask & answer about countries — Where are you from? — I'm from Russia. He's from Japan. — naming countries & flags (Russia · England · Japan · Brazil · France · America).
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time cumulative recycle of earlier levels (incl. instruments L4); folder worksheet (mini-craft — флажок: make & wave a flag); a world-map mingle.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; celebrate I'm from Russia! pride; every child says where they're from every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • ask Where are you from?;
  • answer I'm from Russia. (their own country);
  • name 4–5 countries & match a flag;
  • point out a country on a big world map;
  • recycle Can you play the piano? — Yes, I can!

Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Cumulative Q&A recycle lives at the start (Remember time); the lesson ends on a topic game, then the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello song (chorus), “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives from the bus-box & shakes hands with each child (Hello! How are you?).
  • Remember time ⚡ — cumulative review of EVERYTHING so far: Q&A chain + a quick recall game (Sticky Fingers / board app).
  • Focus time ⚡ — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, 2–3 active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheet, a different type each lesson, always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Final game ⚡ — back to the carpet for a fast, fun game on today's language, ending on a high note.
  • Closing — Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
