🦝 GenkiEnglish Preschool · Giant · Lesson 55 — Where do you live? — forest · river · bridge · farm · hospital · station · castle · supermarket
Genki preschoolers (~6 y.o., non-readers) · 60 min · songs + TPR + games (no reading / writing / phonics) · chairs · carpet · tables · board (Genki app)
Giant level · L55 · Lesson 1 of 8🏡 places · Where do you live?12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to name 8 places (forest, river, bridge, farm, hospital, station, castle, supermarket) and say where they live — Where do you live? — I live near the forest!
  • Subsidiary. Open the Giant level (the last & most advanced) with a cumulative Remember-time recycle of greetings, numbers & earlier favourites — woven into places; the S&S big-book; a "where I live" map craft.
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; a place always in the question (I live near the…!, not a bare list); every child mimes / touches a place every round; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • name forest, river, bridge, farm, hospital, station, castle, supermarket;
  • answer Where do you live? — I live near the…!;
  • mime & touch each place on command;
  • jump to the place Speaky calls;
  • point to their home & the places near it on their map.

Likely problemWhat you do
8 places is a lot at onceTie each to a MIME + a map spot; teach in 2 waves of 4; the song carries them.
"I live near the…" too longChant it as one happy beat; children just swap the last word.
Everyone rushes the mapOne place called at a time; touch it on the big map in a quick rotation.
A shy child won't say whereAccept a point to the map + the place word; grow to the full chunk later.
Transitions get chaoticTrain: Come with me! — one signal, one destination.
Classroom management — signals
"Class? — Yes!" — you: "Class!" → children stop & echo "Yes!"
Volume control 🖐 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
"Speaky says…" — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
"Freeze!" / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — "5… 4… 3… 2… 1…" with fingers shown.
"Come with me!" 🚌 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana's real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🟢 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello / Good Morning song, "choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair".
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the bus-box & shakes hands with each child.
  • Remember time — cumulative Q&A of everything so far (thumb-up/down like a game), growing lesson by lesson, woven into today's topic.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, Genki topic song, board app, active games.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheets, a different type each lesson (map-build / match соедини / colour-by-command / count & stick craft / make-a-clock / draw the hands / draw & tell / sort & stick), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🟢 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
