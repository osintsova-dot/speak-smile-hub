🦝 Give Me Five! 2 (с нуля) · Вводный блок Genki · Занятие 3 / Intro L3 — I, you, we are happy! (pronouns + feelings)
Pre-A1 young learners (~8–9 y.o., zero beginners · линия с нуля) · 60 min · Genki English + Phonics Hero · carpet + desks
Week 07.09–13.09 · Занятие 3✨ NEW · I am / you are / we are~10 pupils · pairs & 2 teams🦸 Phonics Hero ур.3 · c·k·e
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, learners will be better able to say how people feel with the right pronoun: “I am hungry. / You are thirsty. / He is happy. / We are happy!”
  • Subsidiary. Phonics ур.3: sounds c·k·e (+ секрет ck); recycle names & commands inside the new topic.
  • Personal. Teach pronouns with POINTING gestures (я→себя, you→партнёр, we→круг) — grammar through the body, not the board.
By the end, pupils can…
  • point & say I / you / he / she / we / they correctly;
  • say I am (hungry). / We are (happy)! about real feelings;
  • blend and write cat, pen, ten, kit.

Phonics
Phonics Hero ур.3 (тетрадь стр.8–9): звуки c·k·e + секрет ck · блендинг (cat, pen, ten, neck, pet, tick) · «обведи слово» · диктант · tricky said, he — he = сегодняшнее местоимение!
Concept checks (CCQs) · Assumptions
“I point at MYSELF — which word?” → I. “At my partner?” → you. “At all of us?” → we!
“Is it «we IS» or «we ARE»?” → we ARE (big circle gesture).

Assumptions: Занятия 1–2 done: commands, names, RPS; sounds s·a·t·i·p·n. Feelings-словам учим сегодня с нуля через мимику.

Phonics Hero ур.3 — c·k·e
board → desks

Speed reading — стр.8 №1. Strip s·a·t·i·p·n: there and back, beat your time; whisper → LOUD.

Meet c · k · e. c — кастаньеты-крабик пальцами: k-k-k! · k — same sound, каратэ-рука: k-k! · e — разбиваем яйцо о край: e-e-e! The secret (Spiky line): звук /к/ пишется c и k — а вместе ck (neck, tick)! Copy sounds + gestures ×3.

Blend — стр.9 №1. Sound windows: /c/ /a/ /t/ — cat! /p/ /e/ /n/ — pen! … ten, neck, pet, tick — air-blend fingers, picture for meaning.

Trace — стр.8 (desks). Прописи c · k · e · cat · pen, start at the yellow dot, whisper the sounds.

Circle the word — стр.9 №2 (all vote). Picture up (cat) + three words (cat/cap/can) → read all three together → every child points/circles the right one; partner check. Same for pen, ten, neck.

Dictation — стр.9 №3. Segment on fingers → write → Show me! Words: cat, pen, ten, kit. Tricky strip said · he — flash, chorus ×3, air-write: HE is happy! — сегодняшнее местоимение, уже в печати! Medal ⭐.

🎮 Phonics Hub (speakandsmile.ru/gmf1/phonics/, дорожка «Начинающие») — набор «c k e h r m d»: Stations «Звуки» + «Блендинг» on the projector; offline = тетрадь + cards. ◆ Support: dictation with first letter given. ▲ Extension: write neck — remember ck! ✔ Assess: Show-me boards; who blends /к/ twice in neck — recast.
