Experts level · Lesson 5 of 7 (L46)✨ ten…hundred · countdown · blast off!12 pupils · whole-group + shoulder-partner🎧 Bilingual L2/L1 · by ear
Aims & objectives
  • Main aim. By the end, pupils will be better able to count in tens 10–100 (ten, twenty… one hundred!) and do a rocket countdown 10, 9, 8… blast off!
  • Subsidiary. Remember-time recycle of 1–20 & money (L44–45); the S&S big-book; a draw & tell folder sheet (draw your rocket, count the countdown).
  • Personal. Bilingual L2/L1; big numbers always in the rocket game (jump higher each ten); every child crouches & blasts off; ICQ before each game; alternate ⚡/🧘.
By the end, pupils can…
  • count in tens 10→100;
  • do a countdown 10→1;
  • shout Blast off! at zero;
  • say one hundred! at the top;
  • draw a rocket & count its countdown.

Pron. watch

Instruction checks (kept tiny) · Assumptions
ICQ: “Do we crouch DOWN then jump?” → yes, blast off!
ICQ: “Count the tens AND jump?” → yes.

Assumptions: children count 1–20 (L44) → tens are a chant/pattern, not each single number to master; the fun is the countdown & blast off. Meaning from jumps, cards, music & the L1 prompt. Non-readers/non-writers. Attention 6–8 min. Group = 12.

Countdown goes wrong wayShow fingers folding down 10→1; big drum-roll voice; do it together, 3×.
Blast-off → wild🧘 settler after each launch; Freeze! statue on landing.
-ty / -teen mixContrast quickly (THIR-ty vs thir-TEEN) with a gesture, then move on — keep it light.
Transitions get chaoticTrain: Come with me! — one signal, one destination.
Classroom management — signals
“Class? — Yes!” — you: “Class!” → children stop & echo “Yes!”
Volume control 👋 — raise arm = louder, lower = quieter → instant settler.
“Speaky says…” — do the action only when Speaky the raccoon says it.
“Freeze!” / statue — everyone becomes a statue, mid-movement reset.
Countdown — “10… 9… 8… blast off!” with fingers shown.
“Come with me!” 🚊 — line up as a train (паровозик) to change zone.
Lesson framework — Oksana’s real Genki preschool stages

Practice-proven skeleton — reuse in every plan. Bilingual teacher talk (L2 English / L1 русская подсказка). Zones: chairs 🪑 / carpet 🌱 / tables 🪑 / board 📺 (Genki app). Finale = cumulative Q&A chain + the Thank You! song ritual.

  • Opening time 🚪 — door greeting (train), Hello / Good Morning song, “choose the greeting → jump/run/swim/fly to your chair”.
  • Warm up ⚡ — Disco Warm Up song; Speaky arrives on the bus-box & shakes hands with each child.
  • Remember time — cumulative review (thumb-up/down Q&A like a game) with A4 flashcards / memory / app on the board.
  • Focus time — presentation from the magic bus-box, topic song, board app, one active game.
  • Table time 🪑 — own folder worksheets, a different type each lesson (trace-the-route / count & colour / dot-to-dot / sort & stick / draw & tell / mini-craft), always language-linked (listen → do → say).
  • Reading time 🌱 — our S&S big-book (Speaky), read actively with gestures & choral refrain.
  • Remember time 2 + Closing — run the growing Q&A chain, Thank You! + Goodbye song, exit ticket + reward sticker on the folder.
